## Setting

The Pactverse is the shared setting of Wildbow's web serials Pact and Pale. Magic in this world operates through a system of practice and customs enforced by the Seal of Solomon — an ancient compact that binds both practitioners and Others (supernatural beings) to specific rules. Practitioners swear oaths that are literally binding, bargain with Others for power, and navigate a world where spirits track debts and broken promises carry real consequences. The setting spans the mundane modern world, the spirit-haunted Abyss, faerie courts, and demonic realms. Pale follows three girls in the small Canadian town of Kennet who are recruited by local Others to solve a murder.
