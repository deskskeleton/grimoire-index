## Setting

Elden Ring, released in 2022, is set in the Lands Between — a realm governed by the Erdtree, a massive golden tree that serves as the physical manifestation of the Elden Ring's Golden Order. When Queen Marika shattered the Elden Ring, its Great Runes were claimed by her demigod children, each of whom was corrupted by their shard's influence. The player, a Tarnished (one who lost the Erdtree's grace), must recover the Great Runes and decide the fate of the world's metaphysical order. Magic is divided between Sorceries (intelligence-based, associated with the Carian royal family and the stars) and Incantations (faith-based, drawing on the Golden Order, dragon communion, fire monks, and various Outer Gods). George R.R. Martin contributed the world's mythology and backstory.
