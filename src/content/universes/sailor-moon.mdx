## Setting

*Sailor Moon* (manga 1991–1997, anime 1992–1997, reboot 2014–present) centers on Usagi Tsukino, the reincarnation of Princess Serenity of the Moon Kingdom, who transforms into the titular Sailor Moon to fight evil alongside her fellow Sailor Guardians — each associated with a planet in the solar system. The setting layers Japanese magical girl conventions over a cosmic mythology: the ancient Silver Millennium was a peaceful civilization destroyed by dark forces, and its princess and guardians have been reborn in modern Tokyo. The Silver Crystal, an artifact of near-unlimited power tied to Usagi's emotional state, serves as the story's central MacGuffin and power source.
