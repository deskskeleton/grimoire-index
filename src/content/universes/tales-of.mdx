## Setting

The Tales of series (1995-present) encompasses numerous distinct settings (Aselia, Sylvarant/Tethe'alla, Terca Lumireis, Rieze Maxia, etc.) connected by shared mechanical conventions rather than continuous narrative. Magic in Tales games manifests as Artes — a unified term encompassing physical techniques (Strike Artes), magical spells (Magic Artes), and hybrid abilities (Arcane Artes). Each entry provides its own lore explanation for Artes: Tales of Symphonia ties them to mana and Exspheres, Tales of Vesperia to blastia technology, Tales of Arise to astral energy. The real-time Linear Motion Battle System and its variants make Artes feel like fighting game inputs as much as spell selections.
