## Setting

Tamora Pierce's Tortall universe spans multiple series beginning with *The Song of the Lioness* (1983) and continuing through *The Immortals*, *Protector of the Small*, *Trickster's*, *Beka Cooper*, and *The Numair Chronicles*. Tortall is a feudal kingdom where magic is an established part of daily life. The Gift — innate magical fire — is studied at the Royal University and practiced by healers, warriors, and nobles. Wild Magic, a rarer talent, connects practitioners to animals and the natural world. The gods are real and occasionally intervene directly, and the barrier between the mortal and divine realms has historically weakened, allowing immortal creatures to cross over. Pierce uses this setting to explore questions of duty, gender, class, and the responsible use of power.
