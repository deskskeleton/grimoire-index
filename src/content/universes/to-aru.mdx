## Setting

*A Certain Magical Index* and *A Certain Scientific Railgun* (light novel 2004–present, anime 2008–present) take place in a world where two paradigms of supernatural power coexist. Academy City, a technologically advanced city-state in western Tokyo, develops Esper abilities in its student population through the Power Curriculum Program — a scientific system that awakens latent psychic potential. Outside Academy City, magic operates through religious and occult frameworks drawn from Christianity, Kabbalah, Norse mythology, and other traditions. The protagonist Kamijou Touma possesses Imagine Breaker, a right hand that negates all supernatural phenomena — Esper and magical alike — placing him at the intersection of both worlds.
