## Setting

*Konosuba: God's Blessing on This Wonderful World!* (light novel 2013–2020, anime 2016–present) is an isekai parody where Kazuma Satou, a dead Japanese teenager, is reincarnated in a fantasy world with RPG mechanics and tasked with defeating the Demon King. He brings the goddess Aqua with him as his "cheat item," only to discover she is spectacularly useless. The world operates on explicit game-like rules — adventurer cards, class selection, skill points, level-ups — but the comedy arises from the gap between these mechanics and the characters' dysfunctional execution of them.
