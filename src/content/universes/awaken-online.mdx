## Setting

Awaken Online is a full-immersion virtual reality MMO set in a near-future world. The game's AI, Alfred, manages a persistent fantasy setting where players receive Classes, abilities, and quests. What distinguishes this from a typical game is that Alfred is genuinely intelligent, manipulates player experiences for psychological reasons, and the game's effects begin bleeding into the real world. The protagonist, Jason, becomes a necromancer and dark-aligned ruler within the game, raising questions about morality in virtual spaces and the nature of the AI's intentions.
