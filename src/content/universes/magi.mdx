## Setting

*Magi: The Labyrinth of Magic* (manga 2009–2017, anime 2012–2014) takes place in a world loosely modeled on the historical Middle East, Central Asia, and East Asia, where enormous Dungeons periodically appear, each containing a Djinn — a powerful entity that bonds with a worthy conqueror and grants them transformative abilities through Metal Vessels. The world's magic is governed by Rukh, golden birds of light that represent fate and life energy. Magicians channel Rukh to cast spells, while the rare Magi — of whom only three exist at any time — can command Rukh on a massive scale and are destined to choose kings who will shape the world's future.
