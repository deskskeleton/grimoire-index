## Setting

*Edens Zero* (manga 2018–2024, anime 2021–present) takes place across a cosmos of interconnected planets and space stations, where Ether is the fundamental energy underlying all existence. The protagonist Shiki Granbell, raised by robots on an abandoned theme park planet, possesses Ether Gear — a cybernetic modification that allows humans to reshape Ether into supernatural abilities. The story follows his crew aboard the Edens Zero as they travel the Sakura Cosmos and beyond, seeking Mother, a legendary being said to grant any wish. The setting blends science fiction with fantasy power systems, featuring androids, alien species, and time-manipulation arcs.
