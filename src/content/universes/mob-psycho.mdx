## Setting

*Mob Psycho 100* (manga 2012–2017, anime 2016–2022) is set in Seasoning City, a fictional Japanese city where psychic phenomena — telekinesis, spiritual exorcism, astral projection — are real but poorly understood by the general public. The protagonist Shigeo "Mob" Kageyama possesses extraordinary psychic power but suppresses his emotions to prevent catastrophic outbursts, working part-time for the con artist Reigen Arataka's dubious exorcism business. The setting's supernatural elements include evil spirits, psychic organizations, and esper communities, but the story consistently treats emotional growth and human connection as more important than raw power.
