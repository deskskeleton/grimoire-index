## Setting

Disco Elysium (2019) is set in Revachol, a city in the fictional world of Elysium, where a failed communist revolution, decades of occupation, and economic decline have produced a landscape of political exhaustion and personal ruin. The game has no conventional combat — instead, the protagonist interacts with the world through 24 Skills organized into four categories (Intellect, Psyche, Physique, Motorics), each manifesting as an internal voice offering advice, observations, and compulsions. The Inland Empire skill, alongside Shivers and Electrochemistry, represents the game's closest equivalent to magic: a form of psychic perception that allows communion with objects, spaces, and the city itself, presented ambiguously enough that it could be supernatural sensitivity, poetic imagination, or psychotic delusion.
