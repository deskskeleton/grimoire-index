## Setting

Amestris is the central nation of the Fullmetal Alchemist setting. Its alchemical tradition is treated as an applied science, with state-licensed alchemists serving as researchers, engineers, and military assets. Alternative alchemical traditions exist in neighboring Xing and elsewhere, with different metaphysical foundations and partially different rules; future entries may catalog those traditions separately.
