## Setting

Mörk Borg, published in 2020, is a rules-light OSR-adjacent RPG set in a dying world. The setting is defined by seven Miseries prophesied by the two-headed basilisk Verhu — as the GM checks off each prophecy, the world deteriorates further until the seventh Misery ends everything. Magic is rare, unreliable, and aesthetically grotesque, fitting the game's doom-metal punk sensibility. The broader OSR (Old School Renaissance) movement, including games like Old-School Essentials, Lamentations of the Flame Princess, and Mausritter, shares a design philosophy rooted in early D&D: magic is Vancian in origin but reimagined through various lenses — LotFP emphasizes magical research as horror, OSE faithfully restores B/X D&D casting, and Mörk Borg strips it to chaotic desperation.
