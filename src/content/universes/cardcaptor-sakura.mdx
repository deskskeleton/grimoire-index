## Setting

*Cardcaptor Sakura* (manga 1996–2000, anime 1998–2000, sequel manga 2016–present) takes place in the fictional Japanese city of Tomoeda, where ten-year-old Sakura Kinomoto discovers a book of magical cards created by the sorcerer Clow Reed. When she accidentally scatters the Clow Cards, each manifests as an independent spirit with elemental or conceptual powers — Windy, Firey, The Mirror, The Time — and she must capture them using a magical sealing wand. The setting treats magic as a rare inheritance tied to specific lineages (Clow Reed's Western-Eastern hybrid tradition, the Li family's Chinese sorcery) in an otherwise ordinary modern world.
