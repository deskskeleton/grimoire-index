## Setting

*PreCure* (anime 2004–present) is a meta-franchise with over 20 distinct seasons, each set in its own continuity but sharing core structural elements. In each iteration, ordinary girls are chosen by fairy companions to become PreCure — warriors who transform using themed devices and fight villains who seek to plunge the world into despair. Settings range from modern Japan to fantasy kingdoms, but the transformation mechanic, team dynamics, and emotional-power theming remain consistent across the franchise. The series is notable for combining physical combat (PreCure are melee fighters, not just beam-casters) with the magical girl genre's transformation and emotional resonance tropes.
