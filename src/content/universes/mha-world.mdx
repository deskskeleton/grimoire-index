## Setting

*My Hero Academia* (manga and anime adaptation) is set in a world where Quirks emerged a few generations before the story's present, and human society has restructured around their existence. Heroes are licensed and regulated by a national agency; "villains" are people who use Quirks unlawfully; UA High School and similar institutions train aspiring professional heroes. Quirks are universal enough to be expected and rare enough to require classification, regulation, and professional infrastructure.
