## Setting

Sanctuary is the setting of the Diablo franchise (1996-present). The world exists as a hidden creation of the angel Inarius and the demon Lilith, who sought to escape the Eternal Conflict between the High Heavens and the Burning Hells. Their offspring, the nephalem, possessed tremendous innate power — so much so that both Heaven and Hell feared them. The Worldstone was used to diminish humanity's power over generations, but its destruction in Diablo II unleashed that potential once more. Each game's character classes represent distinct magical traditions: the Sorcerer/Sorceress channels elemental forces, the Necromancer commands the dead, the Druid shapeshifts and controls nature, the Witch Doctor communes with spirits, and the Monk channels holy discipline.
