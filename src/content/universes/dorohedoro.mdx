## Setting

*Dorohedoro* (manga 2000–2018, anime 2020) is set across two connected realms. The Hole is a decaying, rain-soaked city where non-magical humans live under constant threat from sorcerers who cross over through magical doors to experiment on them. The Sorcerers' World is a parallel realm where magic-users live in a stratified society, their status determined by the quality and type of smoke they produce — the medium through which all sorcery operates. The protagonist Caiman, a man with a reptile head caused by a sorcerer's magic, hunts through both worlds seeking the sorcerer who cursed him, accompanied by his friend Nikaidou.
