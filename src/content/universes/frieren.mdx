## Setting

*Frieren: Beyond Journey's End* (manga 2020–present, anime 2023–present) is set in a European-style fantasy world decades after the hero party defeated the Demon King. The story follows Frieren, an elf mage who lived for over a thousand years before the quest and will live for a thousand more after it, as she retraces the hero party's journey with new companions. The world's magic system distinguishes between human magic (structured, practical, evolving) and demon magic (innately deceptive, designed to exploit human perception). The Continental Magic Association oversees mage certification, and the setting uses its post-adventure framing to examine magic as accumulated knowledge rather than raw power.
