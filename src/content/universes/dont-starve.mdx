## Setting

The Constant is a pocket dimension shaped by an entity known as Maxwell (later revealed as William Carter, a stage magician who became the shadow throne's puppet) and ultimately controlled by the mysterious Them. Survivors are pulled into this world through a portal disguised as a radio, arriving in a procedurally generated wilderness where they must gather resources, build shelter, and survive against seasonal threats including giant boss creatures, hound waves, and the slow encroachment of darkness itself. The world operates on a day-night cycle where complete darkness means instant death from the Grue-like Charlie. Magic in the Constant manifests through two crafting tabs — Magic and Science — that represent different approaches to manipulating the same underlying reality. Shadow creatures materialize as sanity drops, the Ancient civilization's ruins contain nightmare fuel and Thulecite artifacts, and the lunar island introduces an opposing celestial magic that conflicts with shadow power. The multiplayer expansion Don't Starve Together added the Lunar and Shadow factions as cosmic antagonists whose conflict defines the world's deepest lore.
