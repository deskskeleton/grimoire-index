## Setting

*That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime* (web novel 2013, light novel 2014–present, anime 2018–present) takes place in a fantasy world where a Japanese salaryman is reincarnated as a lowly slime monster. The world operates under a skill-based framework administered by the Voice of the World, an omnipresent system that governs ability acquisition, monster evolution, and the bestowal of names (which dramatically increases a monster's power). The protagonist Rimuru Tempest leverages his unique Predator/Gluttony skill to absorb other creatures' abilities, eventually founding a nation of monsters. The setting spans human kingdoms, demon lord territories, and celestial hierarchies, all structured by the skill system's rules.
