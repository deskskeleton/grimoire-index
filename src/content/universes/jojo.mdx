## Setting

*JoJo's Bizarre Adventure* (manga since 1987, with anime, film, and game adaptations) is structured as nine and counting numbered "Parts," each centered on a different Joestar-line protagonist. Magic systems shift Part by Part: Hamon (Ripple) anchors Parts 1–2, the vampiric arts of the Pillar Men appear in Part 2, Stands dominate Parts 3 onward, and the Spin defines Part 7's *Steel Ball Run*. Stands have been the most influential element of the franchise both inside and outside it. This catalog begins with Stands; Hamon and the Spin may be added as separate entries.
