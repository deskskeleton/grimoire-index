## Setting

Brandon Sanderson's Skyward series (*Skyward*, 2018; *Starsight*, 2019; *Cytonic*, 2021; *Defiant*, 2023) follows **Spensa Nightshade**, a young pilot on the planet Detritus, where the remnants of a human fleet have been trapped under orbital bombardment by the **Superiority**, a galactic coalition of alien species. The Superiority maintains control over faster-than-light travel through their monopoly on **cytonics** — rare individuals with psychic abilities that allow hyperjump navigation, long-range communication, and interaction with the **nowhere**, a dimension between normal space. The Superiority fears and suppresses cytonics because cytonic activity draws the attention of the **delvers**, ancient and immensely powerful entities that inhabit the nowhere and can annihilate entire planets. Spensa discovers she is cytonic, connecting her personal story of becoming a pilot to the larger galactic conflict over who controls — or fears — these abilities.
