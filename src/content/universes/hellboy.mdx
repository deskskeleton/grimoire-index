## Setting

The Mignolaverse presents a world where every myth and legend contains dangerous truth. The Ogdru Jahad, seven dragon-gods of chaos imprisoned in crystal shells at the edge of the universe, serve as the cosmological threat that underpins everything. Hellboy himself is Anung Un Rama, destined to bring about the apocalypse through the Right Hand of Doom but choosing instead to fight against that destiny. The BPRD (Bureau for Paranormal Research and Defense) combats supernatural threats ranging from Baba Yaga to Nazi occultists to Lovecraftian entities. Magic in this world operates through folk traditions that are far more dangerous than their fairy-tale presentation suggests — witches, vampires, and fey creatures follow rules drawn from genuine folklore, and ancient civilizations like Hyperborea practiced sorcery that still echoes through the modern world. The visual storytelling emphasizes shadow, silence, and ancient spaces, creating a magical atmosphere where the most powerful moments often involve a single figure standing in darkness before something vast and terrible.
