**Osten Ard** is the setting of Tad Williams' *Memory, Sorrow, and Thorn* trilogy and the subsequent *Last King of Osten Ard* series. It is a medieval high-fantasy world shaped by the deep history of three non-human races: the **Sithi** (immortal, forest-dwelling, magical), the **Norns** (their northern kin, driven by ancient grief and Utuk'ku's millennia-long hatred), and the **Tinukeda'ya** (ocean-dwellers and changelings).

Human kingdoms dominate the present, but the legacy of the Sithi — their abandoned cities, their magical artifacts, their unresolved conflicts — shapes every aspect of the world. Williams' setting is notable for its archaeological depth: the world is layered with history, and events from thousands of years past have direct consequences in the present narrative.
