## Setting

Noita (2020) is a roguelike set in a procedurally generated underground world where every pixel of the environment is physically simulated. The player controls a witch or wizard descending through increasingly dangerous biomes, crafting magic through a wand-based system. Wands have statistical properties (cast delay, recharge time, mana capacity) and can be loaded with spell modifiers in specific sequences that determine their behavior. The pixel-physics engine means that spells interact with the environment in physically consistent ways: fire spells ignite flammable materials, water spells create pools that conduct electricity, and explosive spells reshape terrain. The world contains hidden alchemical secrets, parallel worlds, and orbs of knowledge that expand the player's understanding of the game's deeper systems.
