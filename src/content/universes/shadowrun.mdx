## Setting

The Sixth World is the setting of the Shadowrun tabletop RPG, first published in 1989. In this timeline, the Mayan calendar's turn triggered the Awakening — a return of magic to a world that had gone through five previous ages of magical activity. Suddenly, elves and dwarves began being born to human parents (a process called Unexplained Genetic Expression), dragons emerged from hibernation, and some humans discovered they could manipulate mana. This magical resurgence collided with a dystopian cyberpunk future of megacorporate dominance, advanced cybernetics, and a collapsed nation-state system. Shadowrunners — freelance operatives combining magical and technological capabilities — work the margins between corporate power and street-level survival.
