## Setting

*Akame ga Kill!* (manga 2010–2016, anime 2014) is set in a decaying empire ruled by a child emperor manipulated by the corrupt Prime Minister Honest. The Revolutionary Army fights to overthrow the regime, with Night Raid serving as its elite assassination squad. The story's power system centers on Imperial Arms (Teigu) — 48 legendary weapons and artifacts created by the first emperor from the remains of ultra-class danger beasts and rare materials. Each Teigu possesses unique abilities and chooses its wielder based on compatibility; when two Teigu users fight, one is fated to die.
