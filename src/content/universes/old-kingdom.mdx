## Setting

The Old Kingdom series (*Sabriel*, *Lirael*, *Abhorsen*, *Clariel*, *Goldenhand*, *Terciel and Elinor*) is set on a continent split by a Wall: south of it is Ancelstierre, a roughly early-twentieth-century country where magic largely fails; north of it is the Old Kingdom, where two opposing magical forces — Charter Magic (lawful, bound, ordered) and Free Magic (chaotic, dangerous, life-corrupting) — shape the land, its inhabitants, and its long-running conflict with undead incursions. This catalog begins with Charter Magic; Free Magic and the Abhorsen bell-necromancy may be added as related entries.
