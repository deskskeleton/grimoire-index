## Setting

Cradle is one of many "iterations" of reality overseen by the Abidan, a cosmic peacekeeping force. The world is drenched in vital aura — ambient magical energy that concentrations into different aspects (fire, sword, life, destruction, etc.). Every living being cultivates this aura as madra, advancing through a ranked ladder from Foundation through Monarch, with each rank granting exponentially greater power. Sacred Valley, where the story begins, is an isolated backwater where cultivation has stagnated at low levels; the wider world contains sacred artists powerful enough to reshape continents.
