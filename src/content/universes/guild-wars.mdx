## Setting

Guild Wars 2 (2012-present) is set in Tyria, a world where magic is a fundamental force cycling through the environment. The Elder Dragons — ancient beings who periodically awaken to consume magic and reshape continents — serve as both primary antagonists and the source of magical upheaval. The game's profession system offers diverse magical traditions: Elementalists attune to Fire, Water, Air, and Earth in real-time combat; Mesmers manipulate perception through illusions and chronomancy; Necromancers channel death magic through minions, conditions, and life force; and Guardians wield blue-flame virtues and spirit weapons. The game's elite specializations expand each profession's magical vocabulary, adding new thematic dimensions like the Weaver's dual-element attunement or the Chronomancer's time manipulation.
