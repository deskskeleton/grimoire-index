## Setting

The Charmed universe presents a world where magic operates through a cosmic balance between Good and Evil, overseen by a bureaucratic hierarchy on both sides. The Elders govern whitelighters (guardian angels for witches), while the Source of All Evil commands the demonic underworld. Witches inherit their powers through bloodlines, with the Warren line producing increasingly powerful practitioners across generations culminating in the Charmed Ones. The sisters operate from Halliwell Manor, guided by the Book of Shadows — a living grimoire that has accumulated spells, potions, and demon entries across centuries of Warren witchcraft. Magic in this world is eclectic: potions require physical ingredients, spells are often rhyming incantations, scrying uses crystals and maps, and individual witch powers are innate abilities that develop and strengthen over time. The show treats magic as both extraordinary heritage and domestic inconvenience, with vanquishing demons frequently interrupting dates, job interviews, and family dinners.
