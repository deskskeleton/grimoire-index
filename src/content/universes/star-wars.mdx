## A single magic system, many traditions

Star Wars is unusual among large franchises in being built around a single magic system — the Force — rather than a constellation of distinct systems. The variety comes from the traditions that interpret and practice it. Across films, television, novels, and games, the Jedi Order, the Sith, the Nightsisters of Dathomir, the Guardians of the Whills, and many smaller groups all access the same underlying Force, but their training, ethics, technique, and relationship to the dark and light aspects differ markedly.

This catalog currently covers the Force itself; future entries may treat individual traditions (Jedi, Sith, Nightsister magicks) as related sub-systems where their mechanics diverge enough to warrant separate analysis.
