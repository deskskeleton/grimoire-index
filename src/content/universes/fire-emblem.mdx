## Setting

The Fire Emblem series (1990-present) spans dozens of distinct settings (Archanea, Jugdral, Elibe, Tellius, Fodlan, etc.) united by shared tactical RPG mechanics. Magic in Fire Emblem is typically channeled through tomes — books that serve as both spellbook and focus — organized by type: Anima magic (fire, thunder, wind), Light magic, and Dark magic, often arranged in a trinity that mirrors the physical weapon triangle. Staves provide healing and support magic, while dragonstones allow characters with draconic heritage to transform. Recent entries like Three Houses introduced Crests (inherited magical bloodlines) and Faith/Reason magic categories, while Engage used Emblem Rings to channel the power of heroes across time.
