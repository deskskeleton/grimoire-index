## Setting

In the world of Iron Prince, humanity has spread across multiple star systems but faces constant alien threats. Every person is implanted with a CAD (Combat Assistance Device) — a biotech organism that bonds to the user's nervous system and can manifest as personalized weapons and armor. CADs evolve alongside their users, growing in power and capability. Military academies like Galens Institute train the next generation of fighters, with students ranked by a public leaderboard that tracks their CAD specifications and combat performance. The series blends xianxia-style progression with military science fiction.
