## Setting

*Re:Zero − Starting Life in Another World* (web novel 2012, light novel 2014–present, anime 2016–present) primarily takes place in the Kingdom of Lugnica, one of several nations in a fantasy world shaped by the legacy of the Witch of Envy, Satella. Magic in this world is drawn from atmospheric mana channeled through elemental spirits, and the plot's supernatural core revolves around the Witch Factors — fragments of power left by the seven deadly-sin Witches that grant their bearers Authorities, reality-warping abilities with severe psychological and physical costs. The protagonist Subaru Natsuki possesses Return by Death, an Authority that resets time upon his death, trapping him in loops of escalating suffering.
