## Setting

The Suikoden series (1995-2006, with Eiyuden Chronicle as spiritual successor) is set in a world where magic operates through Runes — mystical symbols affixed to a character's body that grant magical abilities. The True Runes, twenty-seven in number, are the most powerful and are sentient entities that choose their bearers, granting immortality alongside devastating power. Lesser runes (elemental, support, skill) are commercially available and can be attached or removed, providing the population's general access to magic. The series is notable for its political narratives, 108 Stars of Destiny recruitment system, and the thematic weight placed on the True Runes as objects that drive history through the ambitions and tragedies of their bearers.
