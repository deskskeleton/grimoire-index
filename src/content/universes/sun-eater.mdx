## Setting

The Sun Eater series is set tens of thousands of years in the future, in the Sollan Empire — a sprawling, decaying interstellar civilization ruled by palatine noble houses. Humanity has diversified through genetic engineering into distinct subspecies, and the Empire maintains a feudal structure enforced by the Chantry (a religious institution that suppresses advanced technology). The protagonist, Hadrian Marlowe, possesses abilities that may be psionic, genetic, or something stranger entirely. The setting blends space opera with Dune-like political intrigue and classical epic structure, told as a memoir by an unreliable narrator.
