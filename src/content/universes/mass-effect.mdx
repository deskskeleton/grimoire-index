## Setting

The Mass Effect universe (2007-present) is a science fiction setting where the discovery of element zero (eezo) — a substance that can increase or decrease the mass of objects within a dark energy field — enabled faster-than-light travel, kinetic barriers, and Biotics. Biotics are individuals exposed to element zero in utero who develop the ability to generate mass effect fields with their nervous systems, amplified by surgically implanted biotic amps. Different species have different relationships to biotics: asari are naturally biotic, while human biotics are rare and often traumatized by their training. Though framed as science, biotics function narratively and mechanically as magic — telekinesis, force fields, singularities, and biotic charges fill the same role as spellcasting in fantasy RPGs.
