## Setting

Blades in the Dark, published in 2017, is set in Doskvol (Duskwall) — a haunted industrial city in a world where the sun was shattered centuries ago, the gates of death were broken open, and the spirits of the dead now roam the desolate wastelands between cities. Civilization survives behind massive lightning barriers powered by electroplasm refined from demon blood (leviathan blood harvested by hunter ships). The ghost field — a pervasive supernatural substrate — enables both spectral phenomena and the technologies that keep society functioning. Players run a criminal crew navigating faction politics, heists, and the ever-present supernatural dangers of a city where death is not the end. Band of Blades, a Forged in the Dark derivative, applies the framework to military fantasy with Chosen-granted miracles.
