## Setting

Earthdawn, first published in 1993, is set in the Fourth World — the same timeline as Shadowrun, but thousands of years earlier during a previous age of high magic. As magic levels rose, they attracted the Horrors — entities from astral space that fed on fear and suffering. The Namegiving races (humans, elves, dwarves, orks, trolls, t'skrang, obsidimen, and windlings) survived by retreating into underground citadels called kaers. Now, with magic levels declining and the worst Horrors departed, the people of Barsaive are re-emerging to reclaim their world. The magic system is built around Patterns — the true magical nature of everything that exists — and the Thread Magic that allows adepts to weave connections to those patterns.
