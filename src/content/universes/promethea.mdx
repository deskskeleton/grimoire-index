## Setting

Promethea exists simultaneously as a character, a concept, and a magical treatise. The story follows Sophie Bangs, a college student who becomes the latest incarnation of Promethea, a mythological figure who has manifested through various human hosts across centuries — always through a creative act (writing, painting, storytelling). The Immateria is the realm of imagination where Promethea truly lives, a dimension structured according to the Qabalah's Tree of Life, with each Sephirah corresponding to a different aspect of existence. Moore uses the comic's narrative to deliver genuine instruction in Western esoteric tradition, including detailed explorations of Tarot symbolism, the nature of consciousness, and the relationship between imagination and reality. The setting includes a near-future New York protected by science-heroes, but the real landscape is the interior one — the psychic and spiritual dimensions that Moore argues are as real as the material world, accessible through the technology of storytelling itself.
