## Setting

Susanna Clarke's fiction is set in versions of England where magic is real, historical, and entangled with landscape and fairy bargains. *Jonathan Strange & Mr Norrell* presents an alternate early 1800s where magic was once practiced openly, declined after the Raven King's departure, and is being revived by two very different magicians. *Piranesi* takes place partly in our world and partly in a mysterious infinite labyrinth connected to the same magical tradition. Both works treat magic as ancient, beautiful, and dangerous — something that belongs to the landscape and to bargains with non-human powers, not to human mastery.
