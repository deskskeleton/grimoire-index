## Setting

The God of War series (2005-present) spans Greek mythology (original trilogy) and Norse mythology (2018 reboot and Ragnarok). In the Norse era, magic operates through Runic Attacks — powerful abilities socketed into weapons — alongside Spartan Rage (Kratos's berserker transformation), Atreus's archery and runic summons, and various enchantments and talismans. The Leviathan Axe channels frost magic, the Blades of Chaos channel fire, and the Draupnir Spear channels wind/sonic energy. The world's magical framework derives from Norse cosmology, with the Nine Realms connected by the World Tree Yggdrasil and divine power flowing from gods whose abilities can be claimed through combat.
