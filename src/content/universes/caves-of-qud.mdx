## Setting

Caves of Qud (2015-present, early access to 1.0) is set in the far-future ruins of Qud, a science-fantasy world where multiple layers of collapsed civilization have left behind artifacts, mutations, and fragmented knowledge. Characters choose between Mutants (who gain physical and mental mutations that evolve over time) and True Kin (baseline humans with cybernetic implant slots). Mental mutations include psychic powers like Clairvoyance, Pyrokinesis, Telepathy, and Force Wall. Physical mutations range from Multiple Arms to Corrosive Gas Generation to Wings. The game's procedural generation and permadeath create emergent narratives where each character's unique mutation loadout defines their approach to the world.
