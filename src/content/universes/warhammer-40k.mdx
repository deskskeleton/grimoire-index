## Setting

Warhammer 40,000, originating in 1987, is set in the 41st millennium — a galaxy-spanning dark science-fantasy setting where the Imperium of Man wages endless war against aliens, heretics, and the forces of Chaos. Psychic powers function as the setting's magic system: psykers draw energy from the Warp (also called the Immaterium), a parallel dimension shaped by emotion and inhabited by daemons. The Emperor of Mankind is the most powerful psyker in human history, sustained on the Golden Throne as a psychic lighthouse (the Astronomican) that enables faster-than-light travel. The RPG lines — Dark Heresy, Rogue Trader, Deathwatch, Black Crusade, and Wrath & Glory — explore psychic powers at different scales and from different factional perspectives.
