## Setting

Pallimustus is a world where magical monsters spawn from dimensional rifts and adventurers are society's backbone. Every person can absorb essences — magical aspects like fire, dark, sin, or knowledge — from awakening stones, combining them into a personal constellation that determines their ability set. Adventurers advance through iron, bronze, silver, and gold ranks, with each rank granting evolved versions of existing powers and new resource thresholds. The story follows Jason Asano, an Australian isekai'd into this world, who navigates both the power system and the feudal politics of essence-monopoly aristocracies.
