## Setting

Innworld is the setting of *The Wandering Inn*, one of the longest web serials ever written. Every sapient being in this world — human, Drake, Gnoll, Antinium, and dozens of other species — receives Classes and Levels from the System, an omnipresent metaphysical framework. Classes range from mundane ([Farmer], [Innkeeper]) to legendary ([Archmage], [King of Destruction]), and high-level Skills can reshape battlefields or bend reality. The story begins when Erin Solstice, a young woman from Earth, arrives in Innworld and opens an inn, gradually discovering the System's depths and the world's layered history.
