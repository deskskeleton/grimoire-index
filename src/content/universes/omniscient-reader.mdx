## Setting

Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint begins when the events of a web novel the protagonist has been reading for a decade suddenly become reality. The Star Stream — a cosmic system managed by entities called Dokkaebi — forces humanity through a series of lethal scenarios. Constellations (beings from myth, fiction, and history) watch and sponsor human Incarnations through a coin-based economy. The protagonist, Kim Dokja, survives because he alone has read the complete web novel that predicted these events, but the story rapidly diverges from what he read. The series is deeply meta-fictional, exploring the relationship between readers, stories, and reality.
