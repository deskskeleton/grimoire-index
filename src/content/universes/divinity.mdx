## Setting

Rivellon is the setting of the Divinity series, most notably Divinity: Original Sin (2014) and Divinity: Original Sin 2 (2017). The world features a rich elemental interaction system where fire, water, earth, air, poison, and blood interact physically — fire ignites oil, rain creates puddles that can be electrified, poison clouds can be detonated. Source is a deeper magical energy that powers the most potent abilities but is harvested from souls and spirit, making its use morally fraught. Sourcerers — those who can wield Source — are hunted by the Divine Order, which considers Source use dangerous because it attracts Voidwoken, entities from beyond reality that feed on Source energy.
