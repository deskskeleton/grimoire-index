## Setting

*Bleach* (manga 2001–2016, animated 2004–2012 and 2022– ) is set across three principal realms: the **Living World** (a contemporary Japan visible to ordinary humans), **Soul Society** (the afterlife, structured as a feudal society with a noble-house aristocracy and the Gotei 13 military as its standing arm), and **Hueco Mundo** (a desert dimension populated by Hollows). The cosmology's central conceit is that ordinary souls are routinely ferried between these realms, that the visible world is constantly under low-level attack from Hollows, and that the **Gotei 13** — a thirteen-division military of Soul Reapers led by captains who have achieved Bankai — is the institution that keeps the system stable.

Reiatsu, Zanpakutō, and the four supporting disciplines (Zanjutsu, Hakuda, Hohō, Kidō) are the principal magical traditions. Adjacent factions — Hollows and their Arrancar elite, the bow-wielding Quincy, the Hollowified Visored, and the Fullbringers — practice variant systems that share reishi/reiryoku as a substrate but diverge sharply in mechanism and politics. This catalog begins with the Soul Reaper system; the others are candidates for follow-up entries.
