## Setting

13th Age, first published in 2013, is a d20-based fantasy RPG designed by two former lead designers of D&D (3rd and 4th editions respectively). The Dragon Empire is its default setting — a fantasy world organized around thirteen Icons, powerful NPCs who represent archetypal forces (order, chaos, nature, undeath, empire, rebellion). Each player character has mechanical relationships with one or more Icons, and these relationships generate story hooks, complications, and advantages during play. The magic system combines D&D-style class casting with narrative escalation mechanics (the Escalation Die) and flexible, improvisational elements that encourage players to describe their spells creatively rather than referencing stat blocks.
