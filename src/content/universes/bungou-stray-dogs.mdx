## Setting

*Bungou Stray Dogs* (manga 2012–present, anime 2016–present) takes place in a version of Yokohama, Japan where certain individuals possess supernatural Abilities named after real literary works. Characters are themselves named after famous authors — Osamu Dazai, Ryuunosuke Akutagawa, Atsushi Nakajima — and their Abilities reflect themes from those authors' works. The Armed Detective Agency, the Port Mafia, and various international organizations (the Guild, the Decay of Angels) compete for power in conflicts that blend noir detective fiction with supernatural action. The setting treats Abilities as rare but openly known within the underworld, creating a shadow society of gifted individuals operating alongside ordinary civilization.
