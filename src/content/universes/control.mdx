## Setting

Control (2019) is set in the Oldest House, the headquarters of the Federal Bureau of Control (FBC), a clandestine U.S. government agency that studies and contains paranatural phenomena. The game's magic system centers on Objects of Power — mundane items (a floppy disk, a television, a rubber duck) that have been altered by exposure to paranatural forces and grant supernatural abilities to those who bind with them. The protagonist Jesse Faden becomes the Director by binding with the Service Weapon, a shapeshifting firearm that is itself an Object of Power. The Hiss, an extradimensional resonance, serves as the primary antagonist — a hostile frequency that possesses humans and warps reality. The game draws heavily from New Weird fiction and the SCP Foundation aesthetic.
