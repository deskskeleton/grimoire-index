## Overview

The world of *Vicious* and *Vengeful* is our own — or very nearly so. The critical difference is that dying and coming back can leave a person changed. ExtraOrdinaries (EOs) walk among ordinary people, concealing abilities that range from pain manipulation to self-healing to the control of luck itself. There is no magical institution, no governing body for EOs, and no public awareness of their existence. The result is a setting that reads more like a thriller than a fantasy, where powers are personal secrets and the line between hero and villain is drawn entirely by perspective.

Merit, the fictional city where much of the action takes place, serves as a pressure cooker for EO conflicts. The authorities who do become aware of EOs tend to respond with fear and containment rather than accommodation, reinforcing the series' central theme: that extraordinary power does not produce extraordinary morality.
