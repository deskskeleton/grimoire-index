The **Riftwar Universe** spans multiple interconnected worlds, with **Midkemia** (a medieval-European fantasy world) and **Kelewan** (an Asian-inspired world dominated by the Tsurani Empire) as the primary settings. The worlds are connected by magical dimensional rifts — gateways created by Greater Path sorcery — and the Riftwar itself (Kelewan's invasion of Midkemia) drives the original trilogy's plot.

The setting spans centuries across numerous novels, with recurring characters (Pug, Tomas, the Conclave of Shadows) connecting arcs that range from political intrigue to cosmic threats. The universe's distinctive feature is its cross-world scope: characters, magical traditions, and political conflicts transcend individual worlds, creating a fantasy setting where dimensional travel and cross-cultural magical exchange are central rather than peripheral.
