## Setting

The Persona series (1996-present), a spin-off of Shin Megami Tensei, is set in contemporary Japan where supernatural dimensions overlap with the modern world. Each game centers on a group of teenagers who awaken to the ability to summon Personas — manifestations of their psyche modeled after figures from world mythology, folklore, and religion. The broader SMT franchise features demon negotiation and fusion as its core mechanic, where players recruit mythological beings through conversation and combine them to create stronger entities. Both series share an elemental weakness system (Fire, Ice, Electric, Wind, Light, Dark, Psychic, Nuclear, Almighty) that makes exploiting enemy vulnerabilities the central tactical consideration.
