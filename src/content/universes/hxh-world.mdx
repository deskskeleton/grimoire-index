## Setting

*Hunter × Hunter* is a long-running manga (also adapted in two anime versions) following Gon Freecss and his companions across an enormous, arbitrarily-textured world. Nen is the framework underlying nearly every fight, ability, and antagonist. It is hidden from the general population — most people will never knowingly encounter Nen — but pervasive within the Hunter community and adjacent organizations. The system's distinctive feature is that each practitioner's specific abilities are shaped by personality type, self-imposed rules, and creative interpretation of one of six aura-categories.
