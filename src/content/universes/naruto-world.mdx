## Setting

The setting of *Naruto* (1999–2014) and its sequel *Boruto* (2016– ) is built around a global system of Hidden Villages — military organizations of chakra-using shinobi serving the political nations that house them. The Five Great Nations (Fire, Wind, Lightning, Earth, Water) each host one of the Five Great Hidden Villages (Konohagakure, Sunagakure, Kumogakure, Iwagakure, Kirigakure); smaller nations host smaller villages. The villages compete and occasionally war, and the major arcs of the series concern coordinated threats — rogue organizations, missing-nin clans, and the cosmological backstory of the Sage of Six Paths and the Ōtsutsuki — that force the villages into uneasy alliance.

Chakra is the world's universal currency of power. It animates ordinary craft, runs through the world as natural energy, and is concentrated dramatically in the **Tailed Beasts** — nine massive entities historically sealed into human "jinchūriki" hosts who serve as living strategic weapons. Bloodline-locked techniques (kekkei genkai, dōjutsu) layer additional ceilings on top of the chakra baseline. This catalog covers Chakra & Ninjutsu as the core system; sub-disciplines (senjutsu sage modes, sealing arts, specific dōjutsu) may be split into their own entries in future passes.
