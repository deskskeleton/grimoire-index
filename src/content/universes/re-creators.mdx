## Setting

*Re:Creators* (anime 2017) inverts the isekai premise: instead of a person entering a fictional world, fictional characters are pulled into the real world. The story takes place in modern Tokyo, where characters from various in-universe fictional franchises — a mecha pilot, a magical girl, a fantasy swordsman — manifest with their full abilities intact. The central conflict revolves around Altair, a character born from fan-created content, who seeks to destroy the real world. The resolution depends on the Creators (the in-universe authors) collaborating to write new story developments that can counter Altair's powers, making the act of creation itself the ultimate weapon.
