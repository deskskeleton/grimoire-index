## Setting

*Soul Eater* (manga 2004–2013, anime 2008–2009) takes place in a world where certain humans are born as Weapons — able to transform into bladed arms, firearms, or other implements of war — and are paired with Meisters, fighters trained to wield them. The central institution is the DWMA in Death City, Nevada, a school founded by the Shinigami (Death) to train Meister-Weapon pairs to hunt corrupted souls and witches. The setting blends gothic horror aesthetics with exaggerated cartoon geometry, and the power system revolves around the resonance between a Meister's soul wavelength and their Weapon partner.
