## Setting

*Kemono Jihen* (manga 2016–present, anime 2021) takes place in a modern Japan where kemono — half-human descendants of various yokai species (tanuki, kitsune, yuki-onna, arachne, and others) — live concealed within human society. The protagonist Kabane, a half-ghoul boy raised in rural isolation, is recruited by Inugami, a kemono detective who runs an agency in Tokyo investigating supernatural incidents. The story revolves around Kemono Stones (Nullstones and Lifestones), ancient artifacts that can grant or negate kemono powers, and the various factions — including the powerful Inari organization — that seek to control them.
