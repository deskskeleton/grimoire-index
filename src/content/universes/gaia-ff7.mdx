## Setting

Gaia is the planet-setting of *Final Fantasy VII* (1997), its prequels (*Crisis Core*, *Before Crisis*), sequel (*Dirge of Cerberus*), spin-offs, the film *Advent Children*, and the ongoing *Final Fantasy VII Remake* trilogy. The planet's biology is unusual: spiritual energy (Mako, ultimately the dispersed life-stream of the planet) flows under the surface and through living things, and its concentrated crystalline residue — Materia — can be harvested or grown to grant magical abilities to whoever equips them. Shinra Electric Power Company industrially extracts Mako, with severe environmental consequences central to the plot.
