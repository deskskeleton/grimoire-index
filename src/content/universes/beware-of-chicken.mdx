## Setting

The Azure Hills is a backwater province in a standard xianxia-style world of cultivation sects, spirit beasts, and ascending power realms. The story's subversion is that the protagonist, Jin Rou, a transmigrated modern man, opts out of the cultivation rat race to build a farm. His decision to invest qi into the land and his animal companions rather than martial advancement paradoxically produces extraordinary results — his rooster becomes a cultivator, his farm radiates healing energy, and the Azure Hills quietly transforms. The setting uses xianxia conventions (meridians, cultivation realms, sect politics) but subjects them to comedic and thematic critique.
