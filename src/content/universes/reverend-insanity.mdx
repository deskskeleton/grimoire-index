## Setting

The Gu World of Reverend Insanity is a vast cultivation setting organized into five regions corresponding to the five elements. Gu Masters cultivate by capturing, refining, and combining Gu worms — mystical insects that each embody a fragment of the Heavenly Dao (natural law). The world is defined by ruthless competition: resources are finite, Gu worms are consumed in use, and the path to immortality requires trampling others. The protagonist, Fang Yuan, is a 500-year-old demonic cultivator reborn to his youth, pursuing eternal life with no moral constraints. The series is notable for its villain protagonist and its treatment of cultivation as a resource-management problem.
