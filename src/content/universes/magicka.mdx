## Setting

Magicka (2011) and Magicka 2 (2015) are set in Midgård, a tongue-in-cheek fantasy world that parodies RPG conventions. The game's central innovation is its element-combination spell system: players have access to eight elements (Water, Life, Shield, Cold, Lightning, Arcane, Earth, Fire) and can queue up to five element slots to create spells. The combination determines the effect — Water + Cold = Ice, Fire + Earth = molten projectiles, Shield + Life = healing mines. Spells can be cast as projectiles, beams, area effects, self-casts, or weapon enchantments depending on the casting method. The system has no mana pool or cooldowns; the only limits are the player's knowledge of combinations and their ability to execute them under pressure.
