## Setting

Hydaelyn (later revealed as Etheirys) is the setting of Final Fantasy XIV (2013 relaunch as A Realm Reborn, through Endwalker and Dawntrail). The world runs on aether — a universal energy that composes all matter and fuels all magic. Different civilizations developed distinct magical traditions: Sharlayan scholars practice academic magic, Ishgardian dragoons channel draconic power, Ul'dahn thaumaturges study destructive black magic, Gridanian conjurers commune with elementals. Each playable Job represents a specific magical lineage with its own lore, training institutions, and philosophical approach to aether manipulation. The game's extended storytelling across expansions has built one of the most narratively detailed magic systems in the MMO genre.
