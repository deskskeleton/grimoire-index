## Setting

Solo Leveling is set on a contemporary Earth transformed by the appearance of Gates — dimensional portals that spawn dungeons filled with magical beasts. Humans who Awaken gain supernatural abilities and are ranked E through S by power. Hunters clear dungeons professionally, forming guilds and operating as a regulated industry. The protagonist, Sung Jin-Woo, begins as the weakest E-rank Hunter but gains access to a unique System interface that allows him to level up infinitely and raise defeated enemies as shadow soldiers. The Korean setting and modern-day framing distinguish it from traditional cultivation fantasy.
