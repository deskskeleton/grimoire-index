## Setting

Sel is the planet in the Cosmere where *Elantris* (2005) and *The Emperor's Soul* (2012) are set. Its defining feature is that magic here is **location-bound**: the Dor — the concentrated Investiture left behind when the Shards Devotion and Dominion were Splintered — saturates the planet's crust and is filtered through regional geography. Each culture on Sel has developed its own magical tradition by tapping the Dor through culturally specific means: AonDor (the drawn-glyph magic of Arelon), Dakhor (the bone-shaping monastic art of Fjordell), Forgery (the soulstamp art of MaiPon on the Rose Empire), and ChayShan (the martial-movement discipline of JinDo).

Because the Dor is geographically filtered, Selish magic typically weakens or fails when practitioners move far from their homeland. This constraint — unusual in the Cosmere — gives Sel's magic systems a provincial, culturally embedded character that contrasts with the more portable systems on other Cosmere worlds.
