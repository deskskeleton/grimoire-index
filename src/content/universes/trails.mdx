## Setting

The Trails series (2004-present, also known as Kiseki) is set on the continent of Zemuria, where the Orbal Revolution — the development of orbment technology powered by sepith (crystallized elemental energy) — has transformed society over the past fifty years. Orbal Arts are cast through tactical orbments, devices that accept quartz (refined sepith crystals) in specific configurations to unlock elemental spells. The quartz system means that magical ability is partly equipment-dependent: anyone with an orbment can cast Arts, but the specific quartz loadout determines which Arts are available. The series spans multiple interconnected arcs (Liberl, Crossbell, Erebonia, Calvard) across dozens of games, building one of gaming's most elaborate continuous narratives.
