## Setting

Aurai is a world where magical ability is rare precisely because it demands genuine sacrifice to acquire. The continent is divided among four major factions, each with distinct relationships to magic: the Holy Mission of Elatt channels divine power through faith, the Blue Chamber Collective governs through mercantile pragmatism, the Heroic Kingdom of Levant maintains martial traditions, and the Sorobor Academy pursues arcane knowledge through scholarly study. The player begins as a lighthouse keeper's descendant who owes a blood debt to their hometown of Cierzo, with no special powers, no prophetic destiny, and no innate magical talent. Gaining access to magic requires seeking out specific trainers and permanently sacrificing maximum health and stamina to unlock a mana pool — a one-way trade that makes the decision to become a magic user a meaningful and irreversible character-defining moment. The world is hostile and indifferent, with weather, disease, encumbrance, and sleep deprivation creating survival pressures that ground magical practice in mundane logistics.
