## Setting

The Dark Souls series (Demon's Souls 2009, Dark Souls 2011, Dark Souls II 2014, Dark Souls III 2016) presents a world defined by entropy. The First Flame, which created disparity (light and dark, life and death), is fading, and its decline drives the central conflict of each game. Magic exists in three primary traditions: Sorcery (intelligence-based manipulation of soul energy, pioneered by Seath the Scaleless), Miracles (faith-based invocations of the gods' power, channeled through talismans), and Pyromancy (the primal manipulation of fire, requiring neither intelligence nor faith). Demon's Souls establishes the template with its own magic/miracle split and the World Tendency system that shifts based on player behavior.
