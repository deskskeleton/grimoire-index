## Setting

Monsterhearts, first published in 2012, is a Powered by the Apocalypse game about the lives of teenage monsters. The setting is deliberately contemporary and mundane — a high school, a small town — overlaid with supernatural drama. Each player selects a Skin (a monster archetype) that functions as both character class and metaphor: the Vampire embodies predatory desire, the Werewolf channels uncontrollable rage, the Witch wields borrowed power, the Ghost is invisible and overlooked. The game's central mechanic is Strings — social leverage that characters accumulate over each other — making interpersonal manipulation the primary form of power. Masks: A New Generation (2016) applies similar PbtA principles to teenage superheroes.
