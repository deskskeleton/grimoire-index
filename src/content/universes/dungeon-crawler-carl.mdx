## Setting

In Dungeon Crawler Carl, alien entities called the Progenitors have destroyed Earth's surface and herded survivors into a massive underground dungeon structured as a reality television show broadcast to trillions of alien viewers. The dungeon has eighteen floors of escalating difficulty, each with its own biome and rules. Crawlers receive a System interface granting Classes, abilities, and loot drops, but the System is explicitly designed for entertainment value rather than fairness. The series is equal parts horror, comedy, and social satire, with the protagonist navigating both lethal dungeon floors and the politics of audience-driven sponsorships.
