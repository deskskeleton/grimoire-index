## Setting

Spirit Island, published in 2017, inverts the colonial narrative of most strategy games. Players control spirits — not human heroes — defending their island home and its indigenous Dahan people from European-coded Invaders. Each spirit embodies a natural or conceptual domain (Lightning's Swift Strike, River Surges in Sunlight, A Spread of Rampant Green, Shadows Flicker Like Flame) and wields powers that reflect that domain. The game's mechanical heart is the tension between the spirits' growing power and the accelerating colonial exploitation, creating a race where the spirits must inspire enough Fear in the Invaders to drive them away before the island is irrevocably blighted.
