## Setting

Tamora Pierce's *Circle of Magic* quartet (1997) and its sequels (*The Circle Opens*, *The Will of the Empress*, *Battle Magic*, *Melting Stones*) are set in and around Emelan, a trading nation on the Pebbled Sea. Magic in this world comes in two forms: academic magic, which is studied conventionally and resembles the Gift of Pierce's Tortall books, and ambient magic, which is rarer and bound to a specific craft or material. The four protagonists of the original quartet — Sandry, Tris, Daja, and Briar — each possess ambient magic tied to thread, weather, smithing, and plants, respectively. Winding Thread temple, where the four are trained, specializes in identifying and nurturing ambient mages, whose talents are often overlooked because they manifest as unusual skill with a mundane craft rather than as overt sorcery.
