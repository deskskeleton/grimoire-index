## Setting

The Shadowhunter Chronicles universe spans Cassandra Clare's interconnected series beginning with *The Mortal Instruments* and extending through *The Infernal Devices*, *The Dark Artifices*, *The Last Hours*, and others. Nephilim — the descendants of humans who consumed angel blood from the Mortal Cup over a thousand years ago — serve as a warrior caste tasked with protecting the mundane world from demons and managing the Downworld population. Their governing body, the Clave, operates from the glass city of Alicante in the hidden country of Idris. Downworlders — vampires, werewolves, warlocks (half-demon hybrids with innate magic), and the fae — exist under Accords that regulate their interactions with Shadowhunters and mundanes. The series explores the tensions between these factions across multiple historical periods.
