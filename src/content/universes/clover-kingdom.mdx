The **Clover Kingdom** is the primary setting of Yūki Tabata's *Black Clover* manga and anime. It is a world where magic is universal — every person possesses mana and receives a **grimoire** (a magical book) at age fifteen that defines their magical specialization. The kingdom is defended by the **Magic Knights**, nine squads of elite mages organized in a military hierarchy, with the Wizard King at the apex.

The setting's central tension is between magical talent and social class. Noble families possess stronger, rarer magic and dominate the Magic Knights, while commoners and those from forsaken regions (like the protagonist Asta) are marginalized regardless of their determination or skill. Asta's complete lack of magic — and his compensating Anti-Magic — inverts the setting's assumptions, making him both an underdog protagonist and a structural critique of the kingdom's magical meritocracy.
