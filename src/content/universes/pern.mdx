## Setting

Anne McCaffrey's *Dragonriders of Pern* series (1968–) is set on the planet Pern, colonized by humans from Earth who subsequently lost most of their technology. When Thread — a mindless, voracious organism — began falling from the skies during the passes of the Red Star, the colonists genetically engineered the native fire-lizards into full-sized dragons capable of chewing a phosphine-producing rock and breathing fire to char Thread before it could reach the ground. The social structure of Pern revolves around the Weyrs (where dragonriders live), Holds (feudal settlements), and Crafthalls (guilds). Though the series is classified as science fiction, the dragon-rider bond functions as a magic system in practice: telepathic, emotionally transformative, and governed by narrative rules rather than technological explanation.
