## Setting

The world of Mother of Learning is home to multiple nations, magical academies, and a rich history of mage-craft. Magic is an academic discipline studied at institutions like Cyoria's Royal Academy, organized into shaping disciplines (fire, water, mind, soul, divination, combat, etc.) with clearly understood mana costs and theoretical underpinnings. The story centers on a time loop that repeats one month in the city of Cyoria, during which the protagonist Zorian systematically masters every discipline he can access. Beneath the surface world, ancient dungeons house primordial spirits, and the time loop itself is driven by a soul-magic artifact of extraordinary power.
