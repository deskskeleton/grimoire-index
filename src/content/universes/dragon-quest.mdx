## Setting

The Dragon Quest series (1986-present) spans numerous worlds united by shared mechanical conventions. Magic operates through a spell system that has remained remarkably consistent across nearly four decades: characters learn spells at fixed levels, consume MP to cast them, and draw from elemental families (Frizz/fire, Crack/ice, Woosh/wind, Zap/lightning) alongside healing (Heal), support (Buff/Sap), and instant death (Whack) categories. The vocation/class system determines which spells a character can access, with heroes typically gaining access to the broadest spell selection. Dragon Quest's magic system is historically significant as one of the templates from which the entire JRPG genre's magical conventions descend.
