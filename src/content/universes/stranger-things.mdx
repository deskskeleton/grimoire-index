## Setting

Stranger Things is set primarily in Hawkins, Indiana, where Hawkins National Laboratory conducted MKUltra-derived experiments on children to develop psychic weapons during the Cold War. The most powerful subject, Eleven (Jane Hopper), possesses telekinetic and telepathic abilities strong enough to open gates between dimensions. The Upside Down is a decaying mirror version of Hawkins inhabited by creatures including Demogorgons, the Mind Flayer (a shadow entity commanding a hive mind), and Vecna (Henry Creel / One, the original test subject who became something beyond human). The show's supernatural framework operates at the intersection of government science gone wrong and genuinely cosmic horror — the psychic abilities are products of human experimentation, but the Upside Down and its inhabitants exist independently as a threat that predates and exceeds human understanding. The series gradually reveals that the line between psychic power and supernatural evil is thinner than it appears.
