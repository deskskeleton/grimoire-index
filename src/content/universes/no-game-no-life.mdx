## Setting

*No Game No Life* (light novel 2012–present, anime 2014) takes place on Disboard, a world inhabited by sixteen Exceeds (intelligent races) ranked by magical power, from Imanity (humans, ranked last with no magic) to Old Deus (gods, ranked first). After the Great War nearly destroyed the world, Tet ascended as the One True God and imposed the Ten Pledges — absolute laws that forbid violence, mandate that disputes be settled through games, and ensure that wagers must be honored. The protagonist siblings Sora and Shiro, undefeated gamers from Earth, are summoned by Tet and set out to unite all sixteen races by defeating each in games.
