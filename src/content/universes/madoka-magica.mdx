## Setting

*Puella Magi Madoka Magica* (anime 2011, film 2013) takes place in the modern city of Mitakihara, where the alien creature Kyubey (Incubator) offers teenage girls a contract: one wish of any scope in exchange for becoming a Puella Magi — a magical girl who must fight Witches, nightmarish beings that hide in pocket dimensions called Labyrinths and drive humans to despair and suicide. The series' central revelation is that the system is an energy-harvesting scheme: magical girls inevitably fall into despair and transform into the very Witches they fight, and the emotional energy of this transition is collected by the Incubators to combat the entropy of the universe.
