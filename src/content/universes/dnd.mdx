## Setting family

Dungeons & Dragons is unusual in this catalog: it is not a single fictional universe but a system family applied across many distinct published settings, each with its own metaphysics and cultural treatment of magic. This catalog currently covers the shared Vancian-style spell system that runs across most editions. Setting-specific magic — psionics in Dark Sun, dragonmarks in Eberron, the Weave in the Forgotten Realms — may be catalogued as separate entries in future passes.
