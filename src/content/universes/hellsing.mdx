## Setting

*Hellsing* (manga 1997–2008, OVA 2006–2012) is set in a world where vampires exist as a genuine military threat. The Hellsing Organization, headed by the iron-willed Integra Hellsing, protects the British crown using its ultimate weapon: Alucard, the original vampire Dracula bound into servitude by the Hellsing family. The story escalates from monster-of-the-week vampire hunts to a full-scale invasion of London by Millennium, a Nazi organization that has spent decades creating artificial vampires. The setting treats vampirism as a hierarchical power system with clear rules about creation, abilities, and the distinction between true vampires and lesser undead.
