## Setting

Eora is the setting of Pillars of Eternity (2015) and Pillars of Eternity II: Deadfire (2018). The world's defining feature is that souls are a verifiable, measurable phenomenon. When a person dies, their soul passes through the In-Between and is reborn, carrying fragments of past lives. Animancy — the scientific study of souls — is a young and controversial discipline that can read soul memories, transfer souls between bodies, and manipulate the cycle of reincarnation. The gods of Eora exist but are revealed to be artificial constructs created by an ancient civilization that destroyed itself in the process. Magic operates through soul energy, with different classes channeling it in different ways: Wizards through grimoires, Priests through faith, Druids through nature, Ciphers through direct psychic manipulation of other souls.
