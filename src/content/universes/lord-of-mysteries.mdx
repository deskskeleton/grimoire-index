## Setting

The world of Lord of the Mysteries is set in an alternate late-nineteenth century dominated by the Kingdom of Loen, the Intis Republic, and the Feynapotter Kingdom. Beneath the veneer of industrial civilization, Beyonders — those who have consumed a Sequence potion — wield powers ranging from subtle divination to reality-warping cosmic authority. Twenty-two Pathways correspond to the Major Arcana, each controlled by an ancient deity or organization. The protagonist, Klein Moretti, begins as a Sequence 9 Seer and ascends through increasingly dangerous Sequences. The setting is notable for its intricate mystery structure and its Lovecraftian treatment of higher-Sequence Beyonders as fundamentally inhuman.
