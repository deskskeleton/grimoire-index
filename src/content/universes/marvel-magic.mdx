## Setting

The Marvel Universe contains overlapping magical traditions that rarely acknowledge a single unified theory of magic. The Vishanti tradition practiced by Doctor Strange draws on the power of three benevolent entities through studied invocation. Chaos Magic, wielded by the Scarlet Witch, channels probability manipulation through emotional will and reality-warping potential. Asgardian magic blends technology and enchantment in ways that blur Clarke's Law boundaries. Mutant powers, while explained as genetic, function mechanically as innate magical abilities triggered by puberty and emotional stress. Wakandan mysticism centers on the Heart-Shaped Herb and the Panther God Bast. Each tradition has its own rules, practitioners, and cosmological implications, creating a universe where magical diversity reflects the breadth of human mythological imagination. The result is a setting where a sorcerer, a mutant, a god, and a cosmic entity might all appear in the same storyline, each operating under different magical paradigms that somehow coexist.
