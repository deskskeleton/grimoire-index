## Setting

Tanith Lee's Flat Earth is a gorgeous, amoral, fairy-tale cosmos. The world is literally flat, with a surface inhabited by mortals and an Underearth populated by three orders of demons: the Vazdru (lords of wickedness), the Eshva (beautiful, amoral servants of desire), and the Drin (squat artisans of the deep). Above, the gods are distant and largely indifferent. Magic belongs to demons, demigods, and the rare mortal who earns or steals it through bargains, beauty, or sheer will. The series spans eons and civilizations, told in the register of myth.
