## Setting

Genesys, published in 2017, is Fantasy Flight Games' setting-neutral adaptation of the narrative dice system first developed for the Star Wars RPG line (Edge of the Empire, Age of Rebellion, Force and Destiny, 2012-2015). The proprietary dice generate results on two axes — success/failure and advantage/threat — creating outcomes that are mechanically complex and narratively rich. In the Star Wars RPG, Force-sensitive characters roll Force dice that generate light-side and dark-side pips, spending them to fuel powers while facing temptation to use dark-side results at a moral cost. In Genesys, magic is handled through broad skills (Arcana, Divine, Primal) with spell effects determined by the narrative dice results.
