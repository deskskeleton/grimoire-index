---
name: Navarre (Fourth Wing)
creator: Rebecca Yarros
description: A militarized fantasy nation where dragon-bonded riders serve as elite soldiers, developing unique magical abilities called signets through the bond with their dragon.
---

Navarre is a kingdom perpetually at war, defended by an army whose most powerful division is the riders' quadrant at Basgiath War College. Cadets enter the college knowing they may die — either during the brutal Threshing where dragons choose (or reject) potential riders, or during training afterward. Those who survive and bond a dragon gain access to a signet, a unique magical ability shaped by the intersection of the rider's personality and their dragon's temperament.

The world is built around military hierarchy and secrecy. The kingdom's leaders maintain strict control over information about what lies beyond the wards — magical barriers that protect Navarre from external threats. The relationship between gryphon riders (who channel magic through feathertails rather than dragons) and dragon riders introduces a second military tradition with its own magical culture, and tensions between the two forces shape the series' political landscape.
