## Setting

The Wizarding World is the setting of *Harry Potter*, the *Fantastic Beasts* films, and adjacent paratext. Magical society maintains the International Statute of Secrecy — an enforced masquerade — concealing itself from non-magical populations. The British Wizarding World centers on the Ministry of Magic, Diagon Alley, and Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Other regions have their own institutions (Beauxbatons, Durmstrang, Ilvermorny, Castelobruxo, Mahoutokoro, Uagadou). This catalog begins with the central wand-and-incantation system; potions, magical creatures, and adjacent magical disciplines may be added as separate entries.
