---
name: Codex Alera
creator: Jim Butcher
description: A Roman-inspired civilization where every citizen bonds with elemental spirits called furies, granting powers that scale from household convenience to world-shaking military force.
---

Alera is a continent-spanning empire modeled on ancient Rome, where the fundamental social and military infrastructure depends on furycrafting — the art of bonding with and commanding elemental spirits of earth, water, fire, air, wood, and metal. Every Aleran citizen is expected to manifest at least basic furycrafting ability, and those who cannot are considered crippled. The most powerful High Lords command furies of staggering strength, capable of raising mountains, calling storms, or healing mortal wounds.

Beyond Alera's borders, other civilizations practice entirely different forms of power. The Marat bond with animals in a spiritual partnership that grants shared abilities. The Canim, a race of wolflike warriors, practice blood-magic through ritualists who fuel their sorcery with life force. The Vord, an insectoid hive species, wield a corrupted biological magic that assimilates and repurposes the abilities of conquered peoples. The collision of these incompatible magical traditions drives much of the series' military and political conflict.
