## Setting

Bloodborne, released in 2015, is set in the city of Yharnam — a place built on the discovery of a miraculous healing blood found in the underground Chalice Dungeons. The Healing Church rose to prominence by administering this Old Blood, but its use triggers beasthood — a degenerative transformation that has turned most of Yharnam's population into savage beasts. The player, a Hunter, navigates a single night of the Hunt while uncovering the cosmic horror beneath the Gothic surface: the Great Ones, alien entities whose blood is the source of both the healing and the plague. Magic in Bloodborne takes the form of Hunter Tools — arcane weapons and items derived from Great One physiology — and the Insight mechanic, where accumulated cosmic knowledge changes the game world itself.
