## Setting

The DC Universe treats magic as a fundamental force woven into creation's structure. The Lords of Order and Chaos contest for cosmic balance, channeling their conflict through mortal agents like Doctor Fate and the champions of Shazam. The Rock of Eternity serves as a nexus point where magical energy converges, guarded by the wizard who bestows the Shazam power. The emotional spectrum — the seven colors of light corresponding to willpower, fear, hope, compassion, avarice, rage, and love — powers the Lantern Corps across the galaxy. Street-level occultists like John Constantine navigate a London underworld of demons, angels, and synchronicity magic. Zatanna casts through backwards speech inherited from her father. The Spectre channels the literal wrath of God. This diversity means DC magic spans from the cosmic to the personal, from divine mandate to learned craft, creating a universe where magic is simultaneously everywhere and always dangerous.
