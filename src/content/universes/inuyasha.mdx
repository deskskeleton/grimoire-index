## Setting

*Inuyasha* (manga 1996–2008, anime 2000–2010) spans two eras connected by the Bone Eater's Well: modern Tokyo and Japan's Sengoku period, reimagined as a world where yokai (demons) are a visible and dangerous presence. The story follows Kagome Higurashi, a modern schoolgirl, and Inuyasha, a half-demon, as they gather fragments of the Shikon no Tama (Jewel of Four Souls) — a powerful artifact that enhances yokai and human abilities alike and attracts conflict wherever its shards appear. The setting blends Japanese folklore with adventure narrative, featuring monks, demon slayers, fox spirits, and the shape-shifting villain Naraku.
