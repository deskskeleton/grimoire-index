## Setting

Roshar is the planetary setting of *The Stormlight Archive*, ten projected novels structured as two five-book arcs. The world is shaped by recurring superstorms ("highstorms") that deposit Stormlight — a usable form of Investiture — across the land. Magic on Roshar is dominated by Surgebinding, a system of oath-bound bonds with spren that grants access to ten universal Surges. Smaller systems coexist: fabrials (spren-trapped technology), Voidbinding (Odium-aligned counterpart), and traces of older or lost arts like Soulcasting (technically a Surge application).

This catalog will treat Surgebinding as the primary entry; fabrials and Voidbinding may be added as separate sub-system entries in later batches.
