## Setting

The Warhammer Fantasy setting, originating with Warhammer Fantasy Battle in 1983 and elaborated through the Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay (WFRP) RPG from 1986, is a Renaissance-era fantasy world characterized by pervasive threat, moral ambiguity, and dark humor. The Old World roughly parallels historical Europe, with the Empire standing in for the Holy Roman Empire, Bretonnia for France, and so on. Magic is drawn from the Realm of Chaos — the same dimension that spawns daemons and corrupts mortals — making every act of sorcery inherently dangerous. The Imperial Colleges of Magic, founded by the High Elf mage Teclis, train human wizards to safely channel individual Winds, while hedge wizards, necromancers, and chaos sorcerers practice forbidden variants.
