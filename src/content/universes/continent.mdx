## Setting

The setting of Andrzej Sapkowski's *Witcher* novels and short stories, expanded extensively by CD Projekt Red's video games (*The Witcher*, *Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings*, *Witcher 3: Wild Hunt*, *Witcher 4* announced) and Netflix adaptations. Magic on the Continent is plural: licensed sorcerers and sorceresses (mostly female, formally trained at Aretuza or Ban Ard) draw on an environmental Source via formal incantation and gesture; druids work through nature-bound ritual; priestesses access divine miracles; and witchers — alchemically and magically mutated monster-hunters — wield five battle-Signs as a small, fast, in-combat magic. This catalog begins with the witchers' Signs because they are the most mechanically distinctive.
