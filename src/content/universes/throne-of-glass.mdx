---
name: Erilea (Throne of Glass)
creator: Sarah J. Maas
description: A multi-continental fantasy world where Fae elemental magic, ancient Wyrdmark runes, and demonic Valg forces collide across a landscape shaped by conquest, prophecy, and interdimensional conflict.
---

Erilea is a world of multiple continents — Adarlan, Terrasen, Eyllwe, the Southern Continent, and the Fae-ruled Doranelle — where magic was suppressed for a decade by the conquering King of Adarlan using a Wyrdkey-powered device. The world's magical history stretches back to the ancient conflict between the Fae goddess Mala Fire-Bringer and the Valg, a race of demonic entities from a parallel dimension who seek to consume Erilea.

Three overlapping magical traditions define the world: Fae elemental magic inherited through bloodlines, the Wyrdmark runic language that predates the Fae and can manipulate reality at a fundamental level, and the dark possession magic of the Valg who enslave hosts through enchanted rings and collars. The Wyrdgate — a portal between worlds controlled by three Wyrdkeys — is the axis around which the series' central conflict turns, as various factions seek to seal or open the gate for their own purposes.
