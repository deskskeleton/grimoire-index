**Solidar** is the primary nation in L. E. Modesitt Jr.'s **Imager Portfolio** series. It is a world at roughly Renaissance-level technology, governed by a rex (king) balancing power between the High Holders (landed aristocracy), the factors (merchant class), and the **Collegium Imago** — the institution that trains, controls, and politically represents imagers.

The series spans centuries through multiple protagonists, each navigating the same political tensions from different positions. Modesitt uses Solidar to explore how societies manage individuals with dangerous capabilities — the Collegium's dual role as protector and controller of imagers mirrors real-world debates about institutional oversight of powerful technologies.
