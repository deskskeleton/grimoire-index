## Setting

The Four Corners is the named region containing the bulk of *The Name of the Wind* and *The Wise Man's Fear*. The University in Imre is the central institution for organized magical study, distinguishing this setting by its treatment of magic as accredited academic disciplines rather than mystical or hereditary craft. Sympathy, Sygaldry (rune-craft), and Alchemy are taught and practiced openly within the trained community; Naming is older, rarer, and treated almost mythically even within the University. The catalog will start with Sympathy as the most mechanically distinctive of these.
