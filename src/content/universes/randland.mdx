## Setting

The fourteen-book *Wheel of Time* series (plus a prequel) is set on a world fans informally call "Randland" where time turns in seven Ages. The dominant magic system is the One Power, drawn from the True Source. The Source is split into a male half (saidin) and a female half (saidar) which behave differently mechanically and have very different cultural histories — saidin was tainted at the end of the previous Age, driving male channelers to madness, until the protagonist's actions in the final volume. A separate True Power exists, drawn directly from the Dark One. This catalog covers the One Power as the primary system.
