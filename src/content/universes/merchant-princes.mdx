## Setting

The Merchant Princes universe is the setting of Charles Stross's series beginning with *The Family Trade*. The premise centers on a network of parallel Earths at different stages of technological and political development. A hereditary Clan, descended from a specific bloodline originating in a feudal alternate America, possesses a recessive genetic ability to "world-walk" between timelines. For centuries the Clan operated as medieval merchant-princes, leveraging their cross-timeline access to run a drug-smuggling operation into our world. The series escalates from family-dynasty intrigue into geopolitical thriller as the U.S. government discovers the Clan's existence, and later books explore the para-dimensional physics underlying the world-walking ability and its potential for technological augmentation.
