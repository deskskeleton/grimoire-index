## Setting

Kaldwyn is a world where magic is granted through attunements — mystical marks bestowed during dangerous trials within the Serpent Spires, tower-like structures left by the Visages (god-like beings). Each attunement grants access to specific mana types and a defined set of abilities that grow stronger with training. The story follows Corin Cadence, an academy student at Lorian Heights who studies attunement theory while searching for his missing brother within the Spires. The setting connects to Andrew Rowe's broader Weapons and Wielders cosmology, where multiple worlds share underlying magical principles.
