The **World of Berserk** is the setting of Kentaro Miura's manga *Berserk* (1989-ongoing, continued posthumously). It is a world of medieval brutality where the supernatural is largely hidden behind the mundane — most people live and die without encountering anything magical, while a shadow cosmology of demons, apostles, and the God Hand operates behind the curtain of reality.

The setting's metaphysics center on **causality** — the idea that events are guided by forces beyond human understanding, channeled through the **Idea of Evil** and its agents, the **God Hand**. The God Hand are five transcendent beings, each created through the Eclipse sacrifice — a ritual in which a desperate human sacrifices everyone they love in exchange for apotheosis. The **Brand of Sacrifice** marks those offered in the Eclipse, drawing them to evil spirits and placing them outside the normal flow of causality. Guts, the protagonist, bears the Brand and fights against the current of fate itself.
