The world of the **Saga of Recluce** is built on the fundamental duality of **Order** and **Chaos** — two cosmic forces that permeate all aspects of reality. Magic, politics, engineering, and culture are all shaped by this binary. The island nation of **Recluce** was founded by order mages as a refuge, while the continental powers draw more heavily on chaos magic.

Modesitt tells the Recluce story across twenty novels set in different centuries, deliberately non-chronological. This structure allows readers to see the same Order/Chaos dynamic play out across different technological and political contexts, from quasi-medieval settings to proto-industrial eras. The series emphasizes practical consequences: magic is work, power has costs, and the balance between order and chaos is not a metaphor but a physical law with measurable effects.
