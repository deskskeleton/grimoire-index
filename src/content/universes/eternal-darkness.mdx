## Setting

Eternal Darkness: Sanity's Requiem (2002) spans over two thousand years of human history, following twelve playable characters who each encounter the Tome of Eternal Darkness and learn to wield its three-rune magic system against three Ancients — Chattur'gha (body/red), Xel'lotath (mind/green), and Ulyaoth (spirit/blue) — locked in an eternal three-way conflict. The game's signature innovation is its Sanity Meter, which depletes when characters encounter horrors, triggering increasingly elaborate fourth-wall-breaking effects (fake volume changes, simulated save file deletion, pretend crashes) that blur the line between the character's madness and the player's reality.
