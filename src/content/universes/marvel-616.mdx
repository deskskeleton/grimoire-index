## Setting

Earth-616 is Marvel's flagship comics continuity, in publication continuously since the early 1960s. Its magic systems are pluralistic and only loosely systematized: the Vishanti-empowered Sorcerer Supreme tradition of Doctor Strange, Asgardian sorcery, Wakandan ritual magic, Atlantean magic, Hellish bargains, the Chaos Magic of the Scarlet Witch, and many smaller traditions all sit within the same shared universe. The film and television MCU (*Earth-199999* / *Earth-616* depending on the era) presents a slightly streamlined version of the same magical landscape. This catalog begins with the Sorcerer Supreme tradition (the Mystic Arts) as the franchise's most structured magical system.
