## Setting

In the Super Powereds world, Supers are people who develop superhuman abilities — typically manifesting at puberty. Those who can control their powers are classified as Supers; those who cannot are Powereds, a stigmatized underclass. The Hero Certification Program (HCP) at Lander University is an elite four-year training course that produces licensed Heroes — the only people legally permitted to use their powers in combat situations. The story follows five former Powereds who underwent an experimental procedure to gain control of their abilities and entered the HCP as freshmen. The setting explores the bureaucracy, ethics, and politics of institutionalized superpowers.
