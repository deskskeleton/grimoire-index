## Setting

*Yu Yu Hakusho* (manga 1990–1994, anime 1992–1995) follows Yusuke Urameshi, a teenage delinquent who dies, is resurrected as a Spirit Detective, and is drawn into increasingly dangerous conflicts spanning the Human World, Spirit World (Reikai), and Demon World (Makai). The three realms are connected by barriers maintained by Spirit World's bureaucracy, and the plot tracks Yusuke's growth from street fighter to one of the most powerful beings across all three planes. Togashi uses the setting to explore tournament-style combat escalation alongside genuine character drama, a template he would refine further in *Hunter × Hunter*.
