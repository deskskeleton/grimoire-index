## Setting

Hallownest is the setting of Hollow Knight (2017), a metroidvania set in a vast underground kingdom of sentient insects. The kingdom was founded by the Pale King, a higher being who granted sapience to the bugs of Hallownest through his pale light. The world's magic centers on Soul — a white, luminous substance that can be focused through a Nail (the player's weapon) to cast spells like Vengeful Spirit (a projectile blast), Desolate Dive (a ground-pound), and Howling Wraiths (an upward attack). Charms, found throughout the world, provide passive modifications to the player's abilities. The deeper lore involves the Radiance, a moth-deity of dreams whose infection threatens to consume all of Hallownest.
