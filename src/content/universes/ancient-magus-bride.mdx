## Setting

*The Ancient Magus' Bride* (manga 2013–present, anime 2017–present) is set in a version of modern Britain where magic exists alongside the mundane world, hidden from ordinary people. The story follows Chise Hatori, a Japanese girl sold at auction and purchased by Elias Ainsworth, a half-human magus of uncertain nature. The world distinguishes between sorcery (structured, studied magic that manipulates existing phenomena) and the innate power of beings like Sleigh Beghys, who absorb and channel ambient magical energy from the fae world at the cost of drastically shortened lifespans. Fae creatures, dragons, and ancient spirits inhabit liminal spaces alongside human society, and the College of mages provides institutional structure to magical practice.
