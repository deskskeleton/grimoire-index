## Setting

Thedas is the setting of the Dragon Age franchise (Origins, 2, Inquisition, The Veilguard), first released in 2009. The world is defined by the tension between mages and the institutions that control them. Mages draw power from the Fade — a realm of spirits, demons, and dream — making them both invaluable and existentially dangerous, since every mage risks demonic possession. The Chantry enforces the Circle system, confining mages to supervised towers. Templars, empowered by lyrium to suppress magic, serve as both protectors and jailers. The Mage-Templar conflict is the franchise's central political axis.
