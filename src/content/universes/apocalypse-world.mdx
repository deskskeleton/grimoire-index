## Setting

Apocalypse World, first published in 2010, is the foundational Powered by the Apocalypse (PbtA) game. The setting is deliberately sketchy: the apocalypse happened, the world is harsh and scarcity-driven, and the Psychic Maelstrom exists as a shared supernatural element that every character can interact with through the "open your brain" move. The Maelstrom is never fully explained — it might be collective human trauma given metaphysical form, alien signal noise, or the dying thoughts of the world itself. Its nature is left to each table to define, making it more atmospheric presence than codified magic system. Dungeon World (2012) adapts the PbtA framework to D&D-style fantasy, translating Vancian casting into the move structure.
