## Setting

Diana Wynne Jones' *Chrestomanci* series (1977–2006) spans the Related Worlds — a set of parallel universes that diverged at key historical junctures. Some worlds are rich in magic; others have little or none. The organizing authority across these worlds is the Chrestomanci, an enchanter born with nine lives whose role is governmental: detecting and preventing the misuse of magic across world-boundaries. The series includes *Charmed Life*, *The Lives of Christopher Chant*, *Witch Week*, *The Magicians of Caprona*, *Conrad's Fate*, and *The Pinhoe Egg*. Jones uses the multiverse structure to explore how magic interacts with culture, class, and governance — each world has its own rules, and the Chrestomanci's job is to enforce a kind of magical rule of law across all of them.
