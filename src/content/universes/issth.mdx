## Setting

The world of I Shall Seal the Heavens is a vast, multi-layered cosmos typical of classical xianxia. The story begins in the State of Zhao, a small corner of the Southern Domain on Planet South Heaven, and expands outward through increasingly powerful cultivation realms, other planets, the Mountain and Sea Realm, and eventually the boundless Vast Expanse. Cultivators advance through named stages (Qi Condensation, Foundation Establishment, Core Formation, Nascent Soul, Spirit Severing, Dao Seeking, Immortal, and beyond), facing heavenly tribulations at each threshold. Er Gen's cultivation system emphasizes personal Dao comprehension and the accumulation of unique insights over raw power.
