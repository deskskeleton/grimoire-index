## Setting

*D.Gray-man* (manga 2004–present, anime 2006–2008, 2016) takes place in a gothic alternate 19th century where the Millennium Earl seeks to destroy humanity by creating Akuma — machines powered by the souls of the dead, summoned when the grieving call back their loved ones. The Black Order, a secretive religious-military organization, combats this threat using Exorcists: individuals compatible with Innocence, fragments of a divine substance that is the only weapon effective against Akuma. The protagonist Allen Walker is an Exorcist whose left arm is parasitic-type Innocence, and whose cursed left eye can see the trapped souls within Akuma.
