## Setting

The world of Mage Errant is dominated by magical affinities: every mage has an innate connection to one or more specific materials, elements, or concepts, and their entire magical career is shaped by which affinities they possess. The Skyhold Academy, a fortress-library suspended above the ocean, is the primary educational institution where young mages learn to develop their affinities into practical skills. The wider world features Great Powers — beings of immense magical might — whose rivalries and alliances drive continental politics. Ancient labyrinths filled with treasures and dangers dot the landscape.
