## Setting

The Pendragon RPG, first published in 1985, spans the entire arc of Arthurian legend from Uther's reign through the Grail quest to the fall of Camelot. Players take the role of knights across generations, with characters aging, marrying, producing heirs, and dying over the course of a campaign that can cover eighty years of game time. Magic is rare and largely the province of NPCs — enchantresses like Morgan le Fay, the wizard Merlin, and the mysterious Ladies of the Lake. Player knights encounter magic as something wondrous and dangerous rather than as a toolkit, and the game's mechanical heart lies in its Passion and Trait systems rather than in spellcasting.
