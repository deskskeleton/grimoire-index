## Setting

Tyranny, released in 2016, inverts the standard RPG premise: the Dark Lord has already conquered the known world, and the player serves the victorious regime. Kyros, the Overlord, rules through Edicts — proclamations of magical law that enforce themselves upon the landscape (an Edict of Fire turns a region into a volcanic hellscape; an Edict of Stone petrifies everything within its bounds). Player magic uses a modular spell-crafting system where spells are assembled from component Sigils: a Core Sigil determines the effect type, Expression Sigils modify targeting and area, and Accent Sigils add secondary properties. This combinatorial approach produces hundreds of possible spell configurations from a relatively small set of components.
