## Setting

The Disgaea series (2003-present) is set in various Netherworlds — demon-ruled dimensions connected to human and celestial realms. Magic in Disgaea is characterized by its deliberate excess: spell tiers escalate from basic (Fire, Ice, Wind, Star) through increasingly powerful variants (Mega Fire, Giga Fire, Omega Fire, Tera Fire, Peta Fire), characters can reach level 9999 and be reincarnated to start over with boosted stats, and damage numbers routinely reach into the millions. The Magichange system allows monster-type characters to transform into weapons wielded by humanoid characters. Geo Panels overlay the tactical grid with color-coded effects that add elemental damage, stat boosts, or movement restrictions.
