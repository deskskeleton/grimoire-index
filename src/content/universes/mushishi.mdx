## Setting

*Mushishi* (manga 1999–2008, anime 2005–2006, 2014) is set in a deliberately anachronistic rural Japan, somewhere between the Edo and Meiji periods, where Mushi exist as a fundamental layer of life invisible to most people. Mushi are not spirits or demons but something more basic — organisms that exist near the boundary between life and non-life, operating by rules that have nothing to do with human morality. The protagonist Ginko is a Mushishi, a traveling specialist who studies Mushi and helps people affected by them. The setting is episodic and contemplative, treating each Mushi encounter as an ecological puzzle rather than a battle.
