## Setting

Brandon Sanderson's Reckoners trilogy (*Steelheart*, 2013; *Firefight*, 2015; *Calamity*, 2016) is set in a version of Earth transformed by the arrival of **Calamity**, a glowing red point of light in the sky that appeared roughly a decade before the story begins. Calamity grants random humans superpowers, turning them into **Epics** — but every Epic becomes corrupted by their abilities, growing selfish, cruel, and tyrannical. Cities have been carved up into fiefdoms ruled by the most powerful Epics. Newcago (formerly Chicago) is ruled by **Steelheart**, an invulnerable Epic who turned the city's steel and glass to solid steel. The **Reckoners** are small cells of ordinary humans who study Epics, discover their unique weaknesses, and assassinate them. The series follows David Charleston, a Reckoner recruit obsessed with killing Steelheart, as he uncovers the deeper truth behind Epic corruption and Calamity's nature.
