## Setting

The Ultima series (1981-1999) is set primarily in Britannia, a world ruled by Lord British and organized around the Eight Virtues (Honesty, Compassion, Valor, Justice, Sacrifice, Honor, Spirituality, Humility). Magic in Ultima evolved across entries but is best known for its reagent-based spellcasting system (introduced in Ultima IV): spells require specific combinations of eight reagents, purchased or gathered from the world, mixed according to recipes that must be discovered or learned. The series is historically significant as one of the first CRPGs to integrate ethical systems with gameplay mechanics, with the Avatar's virtue status affecting story progression and magical access.
