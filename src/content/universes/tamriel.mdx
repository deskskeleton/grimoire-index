## Setting

Tamriel is the principal continent of the *Elder Scrolls* franchise, encompassing nine provinces and a deep paratextual history across in-game books, lore, and design documents. Magic is openly known and culturally normal; magicka — the resource powering spells — is a tracked attribute on characters, regenerated by rest, potions, or specific gear. Most spells fall under one of the historical Schools (Destruction, Restoration, Alteration, Illusion, Conjuration, Mysticism, Enchantment, Alchemy, Thaumaturgy — with the official school list reorganized between editions). Adjacent traditions exist: Thu'um (the Dragon Tongue / Shouts), Daedric Princes' patronage, ancestral magic among the Dunmer, and Tribunal worship.
