## Setting

*Overlord* (light novel 2012–present, anime 2015–present) follows Momonga (later Ainz Ooal Gown), a salaryman who is transported into a fantasy world inhabiting his max-level undead avatar from the MMORPG Yggdrasil. The game's mechanics — tiered magic (1st through 10th, plus Super-Tier), class levels, stats, skills, and item systems — function as the world's actual physics. The New World has its own native inhabitants with their own magical traditions, but Yggdrasil's framework vastly outscales local power. Ainz commands the Great Tomb of Nazarick, a guild base populated by powerful NPCs who have gained sentience, and gradually extends his influence across the continent.
