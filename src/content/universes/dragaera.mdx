**Dragaera** is the setting of Steven Brust's Vlad Taltos novels and the Khaavren Romances. The world is dominated by the **Dragaeran Empire**, ruled by tall, long-lived beings organized into seventeen Great Houses, each associated with specific traits and animals. The Empire's magical infrastructure is powered by the **Imperial Orb** — an artifact that provides ambient magical energy to all citizens, enabling widespread sorcery.

The protagonist **Vlad Taltos** is an Easterner (human) living in the Dragaeran Empire as an assassin and crime boss. His outsider perspective — using Eastern witchcraft in a world dominated by Imperial sorcery — provides the narrative lens. Brust draws heavily on Hungarian culture for the Easterners and uses the Dragaeran Empire's seventeen-House structure to explore themes of class, prejudice, and institutional power.
