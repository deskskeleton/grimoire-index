## Setting

Teyvat is the setting of Genshin Impact (2020), an open-world action RPG. The world is organized around seven elements (Anemo/Wind, Geo/Earth, Electro/Lightning, Dendro/Nature, Hydro/Water, Pyro/Fire, Cryo/Ice), each presided over by an Archon who rules one of the seven nations (Mondstadt, Liyue, Inazuma, Sumeru, Fontaine, Natlan, Snezhnaya). Mortals who receive Visions — glowing gemstones that crystallize their elemental affinity — gain the ability to manipulate their element. The Traveler, the player character, is unique in being able to resonate with multiple elements. The game's mechanical heart is the Elemental Reaction system, where combining elements (e.g., applying Hydro then Electro triggers Electro-Charged) creates multiplicative combat effects.
