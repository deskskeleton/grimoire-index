---
name: Prythian (ACOTAR)
creator: Sarah J. Maas
description: A world divided between mortal lands and the fae realm of Prythian, where seven seasonal and celestial courts are ruled by High Lords wielding thematic elemental powers.
---

Prythian is a continent-sized fae territory separated from human lands by a magical wall. It is divided into seven courts — Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter, Dawn, Day, and Night — each governed by a High Lord whose power is tied to the court's elemental or celestial nature. The courts range from pastoral wilderness to subterranean cities of starlight, and their politics are shaped by centuries of territorial conflict, bargains with ancient powers, and the threat of entities from beyond the world.

The cosmology includes primordial objects like the Cauldron (which created the fae and can unmake reality), the Book of Breathings (which can command the Cauldron), and the mortal queens who govern human lands with varying degrees of hostility toward fae. Mating bonds, bargains sealed with magical tattoos, and the inheritance of High Lord power give the world a layered system of magical obligations that operate alongside its political structures.
