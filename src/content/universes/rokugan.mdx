## Setting

Rokugan is the setting of Legend of the Five Rings (L5R), first published as a collectible card game in 1995 and as an RPG in 1997. The setting draws primarily from feudal Japan with elements of Chinese, Korean, and broader East Asian cultures. The Emerald Empire is divided among Great Clans, each with distinct martial, political, and magical traditions. Magic is practiced primarily by shugenja — priests who petition the elemental kami (spirits of Air, Earth, Fire, Water, and Void) to produce effects. The Shadowlands to the south are a realm of corruption where Fu Leng's Taint warps all it touches, providing the setting's primary existential threat.
