## Setting

*One Piece* is a long-running manga (since 1997, with continuous anime and film adaptations) following the Straw Hat Pirates' voyage across an ocean world. Its principal magic systems are **Devil Fruits** (one-time-eat, single-power-per-person, with the price of a fatal allergy to seawater) and **Haki** (a learnable will-energy discipline divided into Observation, Armament, and the rare Conqueror's Haki). This catalog begins with Devil Fruits; Haki is a candidate for a follow-up entry.
