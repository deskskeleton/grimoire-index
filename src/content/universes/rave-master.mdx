## Setting

*Rave Master* (manga 1999–2005, anime 2001–2002) is set fifty years after the Overdrive, a cataclysmic event caused by the clash between Rave (holy stones) and Dark Bring (dark stones) that shattered a tenth of the known world. The protagonist Haru Glory inherits the title of Rave Master and the Ten Commandments sword — a weapon that can change into ten forms — and must gather the scattered Rave Stones to defeat the revived Demon Card organization and prevent a second Overdrive. The setting blends classic adventure fantasy with artifact-based power systems.
