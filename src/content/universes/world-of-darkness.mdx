## Setting

The **World of Darkness** is a shared tabletop RPG setting spanning two continuities: the **Classic World of Darkness** (cWoD, 1991–2004) and the **Chronicles of Darkness** (CofD, originally "New World of Darkness," 2004–present). Both present a modern world superficially identical to our own but riddled with hidden supernatural societies. Each game line focuses on a different type of supernatural being — vampires, werewolves, mages, changelings, wraiths, demons, mummies, and others — each with its own magic system, cosmology, and thematic concerns. The systems share a common rules chassis (the Storyteller/Storytelling system) but differ dramatically in their metaphysics, power sources, and narrative functions. Classic WoD games are interconnected in a shared metaplot; Chronicles of Darkness games are more modular and mechanically refined.
