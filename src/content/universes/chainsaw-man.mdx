## Setting

*Chainsaw Man* (manga 2018–present, anime 2022–present) is set in a late-1990s alternate world where Devils materialize from collective human fear. The more feared a concept, the more powerful its Devil: the Darkness Devil is near-omnipotent, while the Tomato Devil is negligible. Government-employed Devil Hunters and private contractors fight Devils using a mix of conventional weapons and Devil contracts — bargains in which a human sacrifices something (years of life, a sense, a body part) in exchange for a Devil's power. The protagonist, Denji, merges with the Chainsaw Devil Pochita to become a human-Devil hybrid, occupying an ambiguous space in a world that treats Devils as existential threats.
