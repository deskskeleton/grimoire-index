## Setting

*Tower of God* (manhwa/webtoon 2010–present, anime 2020–present) takes place inside an impossibly vast Tower divided into numbered floors, each containing its own landscapes, civilizations, and ecosystems. Residents are divided into Regulars (those chosen by Headon to climb), Rankers (those who have completed all tests), and Irregulars (those who opened the Tower's door themselves — the rarest and most dangerous category). The Tower is governed by Guardians, ancient beings who administer floor tests and control Shinsu. The protagonist Twenty-Fifth Bam is an Irregular whose entry disrupts the Tower's established order.
