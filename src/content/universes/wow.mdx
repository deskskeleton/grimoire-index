## Setting

Azeroth is the setting of the Warcraft franchise, most extensively developed through World of Warcraft (2004-present). The world features an elaborate cosmology organized around six cosmic forces — Light, Shadow, Order (Arcane), Disorder (Fel), Life (Nature), and Death (Necromantic) — each with its own planes, entities, and practitioners. Player classes each represent a distinct magical tradition: Mages channel arcane energy, Priests wield Light and Shadow, Warlocks command fel and demonic power, Druids shapeshift through nature magic, Shamans negotiate with elemental spirits, Paladins combine martial prowess with holy power, and Death Knights wield runic necromancy. The MMO format means these systems have been continuously expanded and rebalanced across numerous expansions.
