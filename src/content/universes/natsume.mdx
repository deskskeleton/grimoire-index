## Setting

*Natsume's Book of Friends* (manga 2005–present, anime 2008–2017) follows Natsume Takashi, an orphaned teenager who can see yokai, a gift inherited from his grandmother Reiko. Reiko spent her isolated life challenging yokai and collecting their true names in the Book of Friends (Yuujinchou), which gives the book's holder power over any yokai whose name it contains. After inheriting the book, Natsume chooses to return the names rather than use them, accompanied by the powerful yokai Madara (disguised as a lucky cat called Nyanko-sensei). The setting is contemplative and episodic, set in rural Japan where yokai inhabit forests, rivers, and abandoned places.
