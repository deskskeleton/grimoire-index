## Setting

The Empire of the Isles spans Gristol (Dunwall), Serkonos (Karnaca), Tyvia, and Morley across the *Dishonored* games and *Death of the Outsider*. The setting is roughly Edwardian-equivalent but powered by whale oil, with industrial machinery, electricity, and surveillance technology coexisting alongside witches, the Overseers, and the supernatural. Magic is rare, hidden, and morally fraught: the Outsider grants his Mark to a small number of individuals each generation, and the Overseers police any sign of his influence with deadly force.
