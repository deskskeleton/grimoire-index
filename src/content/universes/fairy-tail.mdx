## Setting

*Fairy Tail* (manga 2006–2017, anime 2009–2019) is set in Earth-land, a world where magic is woven into everyday life. Mages organize into guilds — licensed organizations that accept job requests from the public — and the guild system serves as both professional infrastructure and found-family social structure. The Fairy Tail guild, based in the town of Magnolia, is notorious for property damage and fierce loyalty. The setting features a wide variety of magic types (elemental, transformation, summoning, lost magic, dragon slayer magic) and escalates through arcs involving dark guilds, the demon creations of the dark mage Zeref, and the return of dragons.
