## Setting

*Toriko* (manga 2008–2016, anime 2011–2014) is set in a world where the discovery and consumption of ingredients drives civilization. The Human World contains already-catalogued foods, while the Gourmet World — a vast, lethal wilderness — harbors ingredients of unimaginable power and flavor, guarded by creatures whose capture levels dwarf anything in the Human World. Gourmet Hunters like the protagonist Toriko venture into these dangerous territories, powered by Appetite Energy (Shokuryoku) and guided by the quest to complete their personal Full Course Menu of the ultimate dishes.
