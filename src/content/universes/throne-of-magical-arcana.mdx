## Setting

Throne of Magical Arcana is set in a world resembling medieval-to-Renaissance Europe, where the Church dominates politically and arcanists (mages) are persecuted heretics. Magic operates through spell models — structured formulas that manipulate reality — and the protagonist, Lucien Evans, realizes that magical theory maps onto real-world physics. The story tracks how introducing scientific discoveries (heliocentric theory, atomic structure, electromagnetism, relativity, quantum mechanics) into the magical framework transforms spell design and upends the power structure. The series is unique in the cultivation genre for treating intellectual advancement as the primary form of power progression.
