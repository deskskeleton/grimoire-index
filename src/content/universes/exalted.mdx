## Setting

Exalted, first published in 2001 by White Wolf, is set in Creation — a flat world bounded by elemental poles (Air to the north, Fire to the south, Wood to the east, Water to the west, Earth at the center) and surrounded by the Wyld, a realm of pure chaos. The Exalted are humans who have received shards of divine power: Solar Exalted bear the power of the Unconquered Sun, Lunar Exalted channel the power of shapeshifting Luna, Dragon-Blooded inherit elemental power through bloodlines, Sidereals weave fate itself, and Abyssals serve the Neverborn dead Primordials. The setting is Bronze Age epic fantasy with anime-scale action — Exalted characters are demigods whose Charms allow feats ranging from impossible swordsmanship to reshaping nations.
