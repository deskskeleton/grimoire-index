## Setting

Moorcock's multiverse encompasses dozens of interconnected worlds and timelines, each home to a different incarnation of the Eternal Champion — a single heroic soul reborn across realities to serve the Cosmic Balance. The most prominent planes include the Young Kingdoms (home of Elric of Melnibone), the Fifteen Planes (home of Corum), Dorian Hawkmoon's future Europe, and Jerry Cornelius's modern world. Law and Chaos are not simply moral categories but cosmic forces that reshape reality itself: worlds dominated by Law become rigid and lifeless, while those consumed by Chaos dissolve into formless mutation. The multiverse is navigated through the "moonbeam roads" and connected by recurring artifacts, notably the Black Sword (which appears as Stormbringer, the Dragon Sword, and other forms across incarnations).
