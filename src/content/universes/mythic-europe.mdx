## Setting

*Ars Magica* (first published 1987, currently in its fifth edition) is the seminal "magic as profession" TTRPG, set in a Mythic Europe that takes the medieval cosmology — Faerie, Divine, Infernal, and Magic realms — at face value. Players typically run wizards (magi) in covenants together with companions and grogs. The setting's central institution is the Order of Hermes, a continent-spanning sorcerers' fraternity with twelve Houses (Bonisagus, Bjornaer, Criamon, Ex Miscellanea, Flambeau, Guernicus, Jerbiton, Mercere, Merinita, Tremere, Tytalus, Verditius) each preserving distinct traditions within the shared Hermetic framework. Hedge magic traditions, divine miracles, infernal pacts, and faerie magic all coexist alongside Hermetic Magic.
