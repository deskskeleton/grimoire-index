## Overview

The world of *The Priory of the Orange Tree* and *A Day of Fallen Night* spans multiple continents with distinct magical and religious traditions. In the West, the Priory of the Orange Tree is a secret matriarchal order that channels siden — a golden, life-giving magic drawn from the roots of the orange tree planted by the Mother, a benevolent cosmic being. In the East, the Seiikinese tradition draws power from starlight and celestial forces, producing a different but parallel form of magic. Opposing both is sterren, the corrupting fire-magic of the Nameless One, an ancient draconic entity sealed beneath the Abyss.

Dragons exist across the world but take different forms depending on their connection to these forces. Eastern dragons are water-aligned and bond with riders in a partnership of mutual respect. Western wyrms are fire-aligned and serve the Nameless One. The tension between these magical traditions, and the religious schisms they inspire, shapes the political landscape of every nation.
