## Setting

*Hell's Paradise: Jigokuraku* (manga 2018–2021, anime 2023) is set primarily on Shinsenkyo (also called Kotaku), a legendary island sought by the shogunate for its rumored Elixir of Life. The island is inhabited by the Tensen — immortal, hermaphroditic beings who practice an advanced form of Tao cultivation, cycling between yin and yang to regenerate endlessly. Death-row criminals are sent to the island alongside their executioner monitors (Yamada Asaemon) to retrieve the elixir. The protagonist Gabimaru the Hollow, an elite ninja, discovers that surviving the island requires understanding and wielding Tao — the life energy that flows through all things.
