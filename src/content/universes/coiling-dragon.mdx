## Setting

I Eat Tomatoes created an interconnected cosmology spanning multiple series. In Coiling Dragon, the Yulan Continent is one plane within a multiverse of Higher Planes and Divine Planes, governed by Sovereigns who embody fundamental Laws (earth, wind, fire, water, lightning, light, darkness, life, death, fate, destruction). In Stellar Transformations, the scope expands further to the Grandmist — the primordial space from which all universes emerge. Practitioners in both series advance through clear power tiers, with each tier granting qualitatively different abilities and access to higher planes of existence.
