## Setting

Hades (2020) and Hades II (2024) are set in a stylized version of Greek mythology's underworld. In the first game, Zagreus, son of Hades, repeatedly attempts to escape the underworld, receiving Boons from Olympian gods (Zeus, Athena, Ares, Aphrodite, Artemis, Dionysus, Poseidon, Demeter, Hermes) along the way. Each Boon modifies his attacks, dashes, or special abilities with divine effects — Zeus adds chain lightning, Ares adds blade rifts, Aphrodite weakens enemies. The roguelike structure means each run produces a different combination of Boons, and the Duo Boon system rewards combining specific gods' gifts for synergistic effects. Hades II expands this framework with Melinoë and additional magical systems including hex spells and arcana cards.
