## Setting

*Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation* (web novel 2012, light novel 2014–present, anime 2021–present) takes place in an unnamed world often called the Six-Faced World after its six guardian deities. The protagonist Rudeus Greyrat is reincarnated from modern Japan and discovers he has extraordinary magical aptitude in a world where magic is commonplace but structured — organized into ranked schools (water, fire, earth, wind, healing, and others) with incantation-based casting as the norm. Three continents (Central, Demon, and Millis) host distinct cultures, races, and magical traditions, and the world's history is shaped by a catastrophic magical event called the Teleport Incident.
