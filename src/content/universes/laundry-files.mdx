## Setting

The Laundry Files universe is the setting of Charles Stross's ongoing series beginning with *The Atrocity Archives*. Magic in this world operates according to information-theoretic principles: certain mathematical operations, when computed, thin the barriers between dimensions and attract the attention of extradimensional entities drawn from Lovecraftian mythology. The British government's occult defense agency — formally a remnant of the Special Operations Executive, colloquially "the Laundry" — exists to suppress unauthorized sorcery, manage contact with hostile entities, and prepare for CASE NIGHTMARE GREEN, a predicted convergence event when rising global computation punches enough holes in reality for the Elder Gods to find Earth. The series juxtaposes cosmic horror with the banal indignities of public-sector bureaucracy: mandatory paperwork, interdepartmental turf wars, and performance reviews that happen to concern the apocalypse.
