## Setting

*Jujutsu Kaisen* (manga and anime) treats the supernatural world as hidden from the general population. The Jujutsu schools, the three great sorcerer clans (Gojo, Zen'in, Kamo), and the cursed spirits they fight all operate behind a dense masquerade. Most of the action takes place in contemporary Japan, where curses haunt schools, hospitals, and rural sites tied to historical trauma.
