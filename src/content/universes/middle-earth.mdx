## Setting

Middle-earth is the central continent of Tolkien's Arda. Its magic is shaped by the metaphysics of the legendarium: the world was sung into being by the Ainur, and what mortals call "magic" is mostly the persistence of those original themes in elves, certain artifacts, and the visible interventions of the few incarnate Maiar (Gandalf, Saruman, Sauron, the Balrogs). There is no formal practitioner caste, no spellbook, and no shared magical vocabulary in the way other secondary worlds use those terms. This catalog will treat the diffuse Middle-earth practice as a single primary entry; the Rings of Power, Palantíri, and elven craft are candidates for follow-up sub-entries.
