## Setting

The Wormverse is the setting of Wildbow's serialized web fiction *Worm* and its sequel *Ward*. Parahumans first appeared after a mysterious figure named Scion arrived in 1982; over the following thirty years, hundreds of thousands of "triggers" — traumatic events that bonded individual humans to alien Shards — produced a population of capes spanning hero, villain, and rogue alignments. The setting trends darker than most superhero fiction: powers come at psychological cost, the underlying entities are extinction-grade threats, and "Endbringer" attacks routinely level major cities.
