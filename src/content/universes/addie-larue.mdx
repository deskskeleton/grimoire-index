## Overview

The world of *The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue* is our own, layered with a hidden mythology. The old gods — beings that exist in darkness, in shadow, in the spaces after sunset — can be called upon by those desperate enough to pray after dark. These gods are not benevolent; they are tricksters and tempters who craft bargains designed to extract maximum suffering from their petitioners. Each deal is unique, tailored to the specific desires and vulnerabilities of the mortal who calls.

Addie LaRue, a young woman in eighteenth-century France, prays to the darkness for freedom and receives it — along with a curse that ensures no one she meets will ever remember her. Her story spans three centuries as she navigates a life of enforced anonymity, finding ways to leave her mark on the world through art and inspiration rather than direct human connection, while the god Luc pursues her across the years, waiting for her to surrender her soul.
