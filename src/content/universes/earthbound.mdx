## Setting

The Mother/EarthBound series (1989-2006) is set in quirky, surreal versions of modern Earth. Mother 2/EarthBound takes place in Eagleland, a satirical version of America, while Mother 3 is set on the Nowhere Islands. PSI (Psychic) abilities serve as the series' magic system, granting characters powers like PK Fire, PK Freeze, PK Thunder (offensive), Lifeup and Healing (restorative), and PSI Shield and PSI Magnet (defensive). The series is renowned for its unconventional JRPG design, rolling HP meters, and Shigesato Itoi's distinctive writing that blends childlike wonder with genuine existential dread.
