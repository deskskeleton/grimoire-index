## Setting

*Slayers* (light novel 1989–present, anime 1995–2009) is set in a world divided by magical barriers erected by demon lords, where human sorcerers cast spells by channeling power from spiritual entities arranged in a cosmic hierarchy. Black magic draws on mazoku (demon) lords, white magic on shinzoku (divine beings), and shamanic magic on elemental spirits. The protagonist Lina Inverse is an infamously powerful sorceress whose signature spell, the Dragon Slave, channels the power of the demon lord Shabranigdu — and whose most dangerous spell, the Giga Slave, calls upon the Lord of Nightmares, the primordial chaos from which all existence springs.
