## Setting

Numenera, first published in 2013, is set on Earth in the impossibly distant future. The Ninth World's inhabitants live in a landscape shaped by the ruins of eight prior civilizations, some human, some alien, some beyond comprehension. The "magic" of the setting is almost entirely technological in origin — nanomachines saturate the environment, orbital platforms still transmit energy, and buried installations contain wonders that function indistinguishably from sorcery. Characters called Nanos manipulate these ambient technological fields through training and intuition, while Jacks and Glaives interact with numenera as discoverable artifacts (cyphers, artifacts, and oddities). The game uses the Cypher System, emphasizing discovery and wonder over combat optimization.
