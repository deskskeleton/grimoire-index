## Setting

Honkai: Star Rail (2023-present) is set in a galaxy-spanning science-fantasy universe where Aeons — beings of immense power who each embody a fundamental cosmic concept (a Path) — shape reality through their influence. The seven combat-relevant Paths (Destruction, Hunt, Erudition, Harmony, Nihility, Preservation, Abundance) determine character archetypes and abilities. Characters draw power from their alignment with an Aeon's philosophy, channeling it through seven elemental damage types (Physical, Fire, Ice, Lightning, Wind, Quantum, Imaginary). The game blends turn-based JRPG combat with gacha collection, set against a narrative of cosmic-scale conflicts between Aeons whose ideologies are mutually incompatible.
