## Setting

Slay the Spire (2019) is a roguelike deckbuilder where players ascend a procedurally generated tower, building a deck of cards that represent their combat abilities. Each of the four characters embodies a distinct magical tradition expressed through card mechanics: the Ironclad channels strength and self-damage abilities, the Silent wields poison and shivs, the Defect commands elemental Orbs (Lightning, Frost, Dark, Plasma) through Focus and channeling, and the Watcher shifts between combat Stances (Calm, Wrath, Divinity) that modify damage output and vulnerability. The game's influence on the roguelike deckbuilder genre has been enormous, establishing the template that dozens of subsequent games have followed.
