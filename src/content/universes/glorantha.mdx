## Setting

Glorantha is the setting of RuneQuest (first published 1978), HeroQuest, and related games. It is one of the oldest and most elaborately developed fantasy RPG settings. Unlike most fantasy worlds, Glorantha is not medieval European in flavor; it draws from Bronze Age Mediterranean, Mesopotamian, and Central Asian cultures. Magic is not the province of specialists — every person in Glorantha practices some form of magic, whether through spirit cults, runic devotion to the gods, or philosophical sorcery. The world's metaphysics are mythological rather than physical; the world was literally created by gods, the underworld is a real place, and heroquesters can enter the mythic past to relive and alter the deeds of the gods.
