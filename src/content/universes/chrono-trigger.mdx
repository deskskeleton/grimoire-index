## Setting

Chrono Trigger (1995) spans multiple time periods from prehistory to the far future, connected by time travel. Magic in this world originates from Lavos, an alien parasite buried deep within the planet. The ancient Kingdom of Zeal (12000 BC) drew power from Lavos to fuel a magical civilization, but its destruction ended widespread magical knowledge. The player characters rediscover magic through exposure to a magical entity (Spekkio) at the End of Time, with each character gaining abilities tied to a specific element. The game's signature innovation is the Tech system — characters can combine their individual abilities into Dual Techs and Triple Techs, producing unique cooperative attacks that incentivize specific party compositions.
