## Setting

*xxxHOLiC* (manga 2003–2011, anime 2006–2008) follows Kimihiro Watanuki, a high school student plagued by spirits, who stumbles into Yuuko's shop and becomes her employee to pay for his wish to stop attracting supernatural entities. The shop exists outside normal space, accessible only to those with a genuine wish, and Yuuko's role as the Dimensional Witch connects xxxHOLiC to CLAMP's broader multiverse (particularly *Tsubasa: Reservoir Chronicle*). The setting's magic operates on the principle of hitsuzen (inevitability) — there are no coincidences, and every supernatural event is the result of interconnected wishes, prices, and choices.
