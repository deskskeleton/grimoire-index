## Setting

*The Eminence in Shadow* (light novel 2018–present, anime 2022–present) follows Cid Kagenou, who was obsessed with becoming a shadowy mastermind in his previous life and is reincarnated into a fantasy world with magic. He creates Shadow Garden, a secret organization fighting the Cult of Diablos — a narrative he fabricated entirely from chuunibyou imagination, except that the cult, the ancient conspiracy, and the sealed demonic threat all turn out to be completely real. The comedy derives from the gap between Cid's oblivious self-satisfaction and the genuine world-shaking events he accidentally resolves. The magic system uses mana manipulation, with Cid's unique contribution being "slime arts" — techniques he developed by compressing and reshaping his body's magic.
