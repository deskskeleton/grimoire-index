## Setting

Tales of Demons and Gods is set in a world devastated by an ancient catastrophe. Glory City is one of humanity's last strongholds, protected by walls and demon spiritualists — fighters who cultivate soul force and integrate demon spirits to gain beast-like abilities. The protagonist, Nie Li, is reborn to his thirteen-year-old self after dying in battle at the height of his power, carrying knowledge of cultivation techniques, rare demon spirits, and political events that haven't yet occurred. The story follows his efforts to use this foreknowledge to strengthen Glory City and prevent the catastrophe that destroyed his previous timeline.
