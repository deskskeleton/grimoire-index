## Setting

*Made in Abyss* (manga 2012–present, anime 2017–present) centers on the Abyss, an enormous pit descending through distinct layers, each deeper stratum harboring stranger creatures, more valuable relics, and a more severe Curse of the Abyss — a force that punishes ascent with escalating afflictions from nausea and bleeding to loss of humanity and death. The city of Orth sits at the rim, its economy and culture built entirely around delving expeditions. Cave Raiders descend in ranked tiers (Bells, Whistles of various colors), and the deepest layers remain largely unexplored. The story follows Riko, a young Cave Raider, and Reg, a robot boy from the depths, as they descend in search of Riko's mother.
