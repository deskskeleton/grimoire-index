## Setting

Prey (2017) is set aboard Talos I, a research station in an alternate-history 2032 where the space race led to first contact with the Typhon — a hostile alien species composed of exotic matter. The game's magic system operates through Neuromods, devices that rewrite the user's neural pathways to grant new abilities. Human Neuromods enhance conventional capabilities (hacking, combat, repair), while Typhon Neuromods grant alien powers derived from studying captured Typhon organisms. The central tension is that installing Typhon abilities makes the station's defense systems — calibrated to target Typhon biology — increasingly hostile toward the player, creating a mechanical cost that mirrors the narrative's questions about identity and humanity.
