## Setting

The Encanto is a magical refuge created when Abuela Alma's candle performed a miracle during a moment of profound grief, generating a sentient house (Casita) and a sheltering mountain range that protected the displaced community. Each Madrigal child receives a unique gift upon their fifth birthday through a magical door ceremony: Luisa gains superhuman strength, Isabela controls plant growth, Julieta's cooking heals injuries, Bruno sees the future, Dolores hears everything, Camilo shapeshifts, Pepa controls weather through her emotions, and Antonio communicates with animals. Mirabel, uniquely, received no gift — making her the family member who can see most clearly how the pressure of maintaining the miracle is destroying the people it was meant to protect. The Encanto's magic is fundamentally relational: it exists to serve the community, but the family's internalized obligation to be perpetually useful transforms their gifts from blessings into burdens.
