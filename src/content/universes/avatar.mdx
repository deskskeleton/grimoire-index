## Setting

The Avatar franchise (Avatar: The Last Airbender, The Legend of Korra, and an expanding body of comics, novels, and film) is built around bending — the discipline of elemental control practiced through stylized martial arts. Each of the four classical elements has its own bending tradition tied to a corresponding nation, and a single Avatar in each generation can wield all four. This catalog currently covers Bending as a unified system; future entries may treat sub-disciplines (metalbending, lavabending, energybending) and the spirit-world adjacency in more detail.
