## Overview

In this alternate history, the world diverged from our own when clairvoyance became recognized as a real and dangerous phenomenon. By 2059, the Republic of Scion — a totalitarian government centered in London — has criminalized all forms of clairvoyance, driving practitioners into an elaborate criminal underworld organized into mime-lords, mime-queens, and syndicate territories. Beneath this human conflict lies a deeper secret: the Rephaim, an ancient race from another plane of existence, have struck a deal with Scion to receive clairvoyant prisoners in exchange for protection from the Emim, monstrous creatures that threaten both species.

The aether — a spirit realm overlaying the physical world — is the source of all clairvoyant power. It is populated by spirits of the dead, and the boundary between it and the corporeal world can be crossed, manipulated, and weaponized by those with the gift. The city of Oxford, known as Sheol I, serves as the Rephaim's colony where captured clairvoyants are forced to serve as soldiers and aura sources.
