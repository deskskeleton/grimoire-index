## Setting

*Witch Hat Atelier* (manga 2016–present) takes place in a world where magic was once believed to be an innate gift but is actually a craft — anyone who learns the secret of drawing spell-glyphs with enchanted ink can perform magic. The Pointed-Hat Witches maintain this secret by erasing the memories of outsiders who discover it, preserving a social order in which only initiated witches practice. The protagonist Coco, a girl from a non-magical family, discovers the truth and enters an atelier to study under the witch Qifrey. The setting's central tension is the Forbidden Arts — spells that affect the human body, banned after a historical catastrophe — and the underground Brimmed Caps who practice them.
