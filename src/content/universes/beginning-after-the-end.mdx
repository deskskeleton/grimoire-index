## Setting

The Beginning After the End is set in the continent of Dicathen, home to humans, elves, dwarves, and beast-kin, and the hostile continent of Alacrya across the sea. Mages develop mana cores that progress through color-coded stages (black, red, orange, yellow, silver, white), with each stage granting greater mana capacity and control. The protagonist, Arthur Leywin, is a reincarnated king from a modern Earth-like world (Elshire) who retains his memories and combat instincts. The story escalates from academy training to continental war to a cosmic conflict between the god-like Asuras and Vritra clans.
